## Expansion Guidelines

When creating character entries, follow these principles:

1. **Use the Character Template** from AGENTS.md
2. **Include 3-5 cross-references** to locations, events, factions, or other characters
3. **Match filename to H1** (e.g., `throndor-the-wise.md` → `# Throndor the Wise`)
4. **Establish clear motivations** and relationships
5. **Connect to historical events** where appropriate

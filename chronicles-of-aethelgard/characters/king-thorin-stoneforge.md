# King Thorin Stoneforge

## Overview

King Thorin Stoneforge is the current High King of the Dwarves, ruling from **Stonehold** in the Stormpeak Mountains. A descendant of **Grimjaw Ironhand** and the Stoneforge clan, Thorin has led the dwarven people through the post-war era with wisdom, strength, and a vision for dwarven resurgence. Known for his exceptional craftsmanship, diplomatic skill, and commitment to dwarven traditions while embracing necessary change, Thorin represents the best of dwarven leadership.

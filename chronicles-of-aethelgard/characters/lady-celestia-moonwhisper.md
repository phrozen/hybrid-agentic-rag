# Lady Celestia Moonwhisper

## Overview

Lady Celestia Moonwhisper is the High Ambassador of Silvermoon and the primary diplomatic representative of the elven people to the wider world. A High Elf of remarkable beauty, wisdom, and political acumen, she has served as the architect of elven re-engagement with other species since the end of the **War of Shattered Skies**. As head of the **High Elf Council of Silvermoon**, she effectively rules the elven kingdom while working to bridge the gap between elven isolationism and the necessities of modern diplomacy.

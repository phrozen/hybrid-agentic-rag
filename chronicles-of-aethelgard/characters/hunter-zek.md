# Hunter Zek

## Overview

Hunter Zek is a **Lizardfolk** guide and explorer specializing in **Southern Expanse** swamp and jungle territories, particularly the **Bogs** surrounding **Ssithik's Landing**. His knowledge of dangerous terrain, exotic creatures, and hidden ruins makes him invaluable to **Arcane College** researchers, **Catfolk** traders, and adventurous merchants. Zek worked with **Chief Ssithik** during the **Lizardfolk Unification**, using his reputation among tribes to convince holdouts, and maintains relationships with **Shaman Vessska** for spiritual guidance.

## Background and Origins

Born in the **Ssithik Swamp** in 425 AS to the Blackscale tribe, Zek displayed exceptional tracking abilities from hatching. His clutch-mother was a renowned hunter who taught him swamp survival, creature behavior, and ancient **Lizardfolk** tracking techniques.

Zek's first solo hunt at age 10 resulted in capturing a venomous swamp serpent bare-handed, earning him adult status early. His reputation grew through successful hunts and dangerous expeditions into uncharted jungle regions.

## Role and Significance

### Premier Swamp Guide

Zek guides researchers, merchants, and adventurers through **Southern Expanse** wetlands. His clients include **Arcane College of Thaldor** expeditions seeking ancient ruins, **Catfolk** traders establishing new routes, and **Queen Elara**'s scouts mapping territories.

### Tribal Liaison

Zek serves as intermediary between **Lizardfolk** tribes and surface species. **Chief Ssithik** appointed him official ambassador after his unification work. He negotiates safe passage agreements and trade access.

### Ruins Expert

Zek has discovered dozens of ancient **Lizardfolk** ruins, sharing findings with **Arcane College** scholars. His maps of **Shadowfen** and **Bogs** are standard references for explorers.

## Relationships and Allies

### Chief Ssithik

Zek and **Chief Ssithik** collaborated during the **Lizardfolk Unification** (440-445 AS). Zek's reputation among isolated tribes convinced holdouts to join the confederation. **Chief Ssithik** trusts Zek with sensitive diplomatic missions.

### Shaman Vessska

**Shaman Vessska** provides Zek spiritual guidance and creature lore. Their partnership combines practical survival knowledge with ancestral wisdom. Zek consults her before entering sacred sites.

### Merchant Prince Whiskerwind

**Catfolk** trader employs Zek to scout new routes through **Southern Expanse**. Their business relationship has yielded profitable jungle trade networks. **Whiskerwind** considers Zek indispensable.

### Arcane College Researchers

**Thaldor** mages hire Zek for ruin expeditions. His discoveries have advanced understanding of ancient **Lizardfolk** magic. **Throndor the Wise** personally thanked Zek for recovering lost texts.

## Abilities and Traits

### Master Tracker

Zek reads tracks, trails, and environmental signs with supernatural skill. He can identify creatures by scent, predict weather from humidity, and navigate without landmarks.

### Swamp Survival

Zek survives indefinitely in wetlands, finding food, water, and shelter. His knowledge of poisonous plants, dangerous creatures, and safe zones keeps clients alive.

### Creature Communication

Zek understands **Lizardfolk** beast-speech, calming or redirecting dangerous animals. His reputation among swamp creatures reduces attack risks.

### Combat Expertise

Zek fights with spear, blowgun, and stone axe. His guerrilla tactics in swamp terrain have defeated larger forces. **Admiral Hornbreaker** recruited Zek for coastal defense planning.

## Notable Deeds

- **Guided Thaldor Expedition** (448 AS): Discovered ancient **Lizardfolk** temple in **Shadowfen**
- **Negotiated Tribe Access** (443 AS): Convinced holdout tribes to join **Chief Ssithik**'s confederation
- **Mapped the Bogs** (446 AS): Created definitive maps of **Ssithik's Landing** surroundings
- **Rescued Catfolk Caravan** (450 AS): Saved **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s traders from swamp beasts
- **Discovered Ruin Library** (452 AS): Found ancient **Lizardfolk** texts now studied at **Arcane College**
- **Advised Naval Defense** (451 AS): Consulted with **Admiral Hornbreaker** on **Southern Expanse** coastal security

## Mentions In Lore

Zek's reputation extends across Aethelgard:

- **Arcane College** references his expedition journals
- **Catfolk** traders share "Zek's Route" as safest jungle path
- **Chief Ssithik** cites him as model **Lizardfolk**-surface cooperation
- **Queen Elara**'s scouts study his mapping techniques
- **Shaman Vessska** includes his discoveries in tribal oral history

Zek continues guiding expeditions, training younger **Lizardfolk** trackers, and expanding knowledge of **Southern Expanse** territories.

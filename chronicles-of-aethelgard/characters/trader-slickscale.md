# Trader Slickscale

## Overview

Trader Slickscale is a prominent **Lizardfolk** merchant operating from **Rivermouth**, **Southern Expanse**, specializing in exotic goods from swamp territories and coordinating trade between **Lizardfolk**, **Dragonborn**, and surface merchants. His trading network extends to **Crystal Bay** and **Silverhaven**, dealing in medicinal herbs, rare woods, and **Aqualonis** salvage. Slickscale supported **Chief Ssithik**'s **Lizardfolk Unification** by demonstrating economic benefits of unified trade policy, and maintains **Order of the Silver Dawn** contacts for ethical business practices.

## Background and Origins

Born in the **Ssithik Swamp** in 418 AS to the Slickscale trading clan, Slickscale (birth name: Ssk'thik Slickscale) learned commerce from his clutch-parents, who operated river boats trading between isolated **Lizardfolk** villages.

Slickscale's first independent trade voyage at age 12 resulted in profitable exchanges of swamp herbs for **Dwarven** metal tools. His reputation grew through fair dealing and reliable deliveries, rare traits in frontier trade.

## Role and Significance

### Primary Lizardfolk Merchant

Slickscale serves as main commercial link between **Lizardfolk** territories and surface markets. His warehouses in **Rivermouth** store medicinal herbs, rare woods, and exotic crafts before distribution to **Silverhaven**, **Crystal Bay**, and **Thaldor**.

### Aqualonis Salvage Operator

Slickscale holds exclusive **Chief Ssithik**-granted rights to salvage **Aqualonis**, the sunken city. His divers recover ancient **Lizardfolk** artifacts, selling some to **Arcane College of Thaldor** researchers and keeping others for **Lizardfolk** cultural preservation.

### Unification Economic Advisor

During the **Lizardfolk Unification** (430-445 AS), Slickscale demonstrated to tribal chiefs how unified trade policy would increase prosperity. His economic arguments convinced holdout tribes more effectively than **Chief Ssithik**'s diplomatic appeals.

## Relationships and Allies

### Chief Ssithik

Slickscale and **Chief Ssithik** maintain close partnership. **Chief Ssithik** granted Slickscale salvage rights and trade monopolies in exchange for economic development expertise. Their collaboration exemplifies **Lizardfolk** pragmatic cooperation.

### Hunter Zek

**Lizardfolk** guide **Hunter Zek** scouts safe routes for Slickscale's caravans through **Southern Expanse** swamps. Their business arrangement ensures reliable deliveries despite dangerous terrain.

### Merchant Prince Whiskerwind

**Catfolk** trader and Slickscale developed mutually beneficial partnership. **Whiskerwind**'s surface distribution networks connect to Slickscale's **Lizardfolk** supply sources. Their joint ventures dominate southern herbal medicine markets.

### Shaman Vessska

Slickscale consults **Shaman Vessska** before entering sacred **Aqualonis** zones. Her spiritual guidance prevents desecration while allowing profitable salvage operations.

## Abilities and Traits

### Master Negotiator

Slickscale negotiates favorable terms through patience, cultural understanding, and reputation for honesty. His deals satisfy all parties, creating long-term partnerships rather than one-time transactions.

### Swamp Navigation

Slickscale navigates **Southern Expanse** wetlands as skillfully as **Hunter Zek**. His river boats reach isolated villages other merchants can't access, giving him monopoly on certain trade routes.

### Appraisal Expertise

Slickscale identifies valuable artifacts, herbs, and materials with supernatural accuracy. **Arcane College** researchers trust his appraisals for ancient **Lizardfolk** items.

### Multilingual Trader

Slickscale speaks **Lizardfolk**, Common, **Catfolk** trade-tongue, and **Dragonborn** commercial language. His linguistic skills bridge cultural gaps in complex multi-species negotiations.

## Notable Deeds

- **Funded Unification** (435 AS): Provided **Chief Ssithik** with economic development plan
- **Discovered Aqualonis Entrance** (440 AS): Located main gate to sunken city
- **Established Rivermouth Trading Post** (438 AS): Built **Southern Expanse** commercial hub
- **Negotiated Herbal Monopoly** (442 AS): Exclusive rights to swamp medicinal herb trade
- **Supplied Thaldor Researchers** (445 AS): Provided **Arcane College** with **Aqualonis** artifacts
- **Mediated Trade Dispute** (448 AS): Resolved **Dragonborn**-**Lizardfolk** commercial conflict peacefully

## Mentions In Lore

Slickscale's reputation extends across Aethelgard:

- **Arcane College of Thaldor** lists him as preferred **Lizardfolk** artifact supplier
- **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** calls Slickscale "most reliable southern partner"
- **Chief Ssithik** appointed him official **Lizardfolk** trade ambassador
- **Order of the Silver Dawn** recognizes his ethical business practices
- **Hunter Zek** credits Slickscale for funding swamp exploration expeditions
- **Lady Zara Goldscale** consults him on **Dragonborn**-**Lizardfolk** trade matters

Slickscale continues expanding trade networks, training younger **Lizardfolk** merchants, and preserving **Aqualonis** cultural heritage through selective artifact sales and museum donations.

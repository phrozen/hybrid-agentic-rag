# Founding of Silverhaven

## Overview

The Founding of Silverhaven occurred in 127 AS, when human refugees from the Eastern Plains established a fortified settlement that would grow to become the greatest city in the Eastern Kingdoms. Originally a desperate refuge for survivors of tribal conflicts and monster raids, Silverhaven's strategic location, natural harbor, and defensible position enabled it to flourish into a major trade hub and eventually the political center of the unified Eastern Kingdoms under **King Aldric the Unifier**.

## Date and Era

- **Founded**: 127 AS (After Sundering)
- **Era**: Age of Expansion (50-250 AS)
- **Founder**: Chieftain Aldric (later **King Aldric the Unifier**)
- **Original Purpose**: Refugee settlement and trading post
- **Modern Status**: Capital of Eastern Kingdoms, largest city in Aethelgard

# The Sundering

## Overview

The Sundering was the cataclysmic event that occurred in Year 0 AS (After Sundering), resetting the calendar and fundamentally reshaping the world of Aethelgard. This magical and geological catastrophe ended the Age of Dragons, destroyed ancient civilizations, and created the conditions for the rise of modern species and kingdoms. The exact cause remains debated, but its effects define the world to this day.

## Date and Era

- **Occurrence**: Year 0 AS (calendar epoch)
- **Era**: End of Age of Dragons, beginning of Age of Sundering
- **Duration**: Event lasted approximately 30 days, effects continued for centuries
- **Calendar**: All modern calendars date from the Sundering

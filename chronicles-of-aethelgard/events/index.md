## Expansion Guidelines

When creating event entries, follow these principles:

1. **Use the Event Template** from AGENTS.md
2. **Include 3-5 cross-references** to characters, locations, factions, or other events
3. **Match filename to H1** (e.g., `siege-of-silverhaven.md` → `# Siege of Silverhaven`)
4. **Describe causes, key moments, and consequences**
5. **Connect to broader historical narrative**

### Event Density Targets

**Current Status**: 20+ event entries complete covering major historical moments

- ✅ **Phase 4 Complete**: Major events (20+ total)

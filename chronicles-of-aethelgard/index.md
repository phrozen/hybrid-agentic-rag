## World Overview

### The World of Aethelgard

Aethelgard is a vast fantasy world composed of multiple continents, each with unique cultures, ecosystems, and magical traditions. The world has existed for thousands of years, marked by distinct eras defined by major historical events and cosmic changes.

### Major Continents

- **Eastern Kingdoms**: Human-dominated lands with diverse multi-species cities
- **Western Reaches**: Ancient elven forests and dwarven mountain holds
- **Southern Expanse**: Tropical regions home to lizardfolk and dragonborn
- **Northern Wastes**: Harsh tundra and frozen mountains
- **The Shattered Isles**: Archipelago of mysterious islands

### Current Era

The world currently exists in the **Age of Splendor** (approximately 500 AS - After Sundering), a time of relative peace and prosperity following the catastrophic **War of Shattered Skies**. Trade flourishes between nations, though old tensions simmer beneath the surface.

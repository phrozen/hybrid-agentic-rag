# The Wine Merchants' Consortium

## Overview

The Wine Merchants' Consortium controls viticulture and wine trade across Aethelgard, with **Eastern Plains** vineyards, **Western Reaches** elven vintages, and **Southern Expanse** tropical wines all represented. The Consortium sets quality standards, coordinates harvest timing, and manages international distribution. **Queen Elara** and **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** both patronize the Consortium, whose products feature prominently in diplomatic functions.

## Founding and History

The Wine Merchants' Consortium was founded in 340 AS when Aethelgard's growing wine trade required organized quality standards, harvest coordination, and international distribution. Individual vineyard owners and wine merchants could no longer manage the complexities of cross-kingdom wine commerce.

### Founding Period (340-370 AS)

**Trade Organization**: Human wine merchant **Vintage Copperwell** and elven vintner **Silverleaf Sunshadow** co-founded the consortium to unite **Eastern Plains** vineyard owners, **Western Reaches** elven vintners, and wine merchants under quality standards and fair trade practices. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s predecessor endorsed elven participation.

**Quality Standards**: The consortium established Aethelgard's first wine quality classification system—vintage years, regional appellations, and quality tiers. **Queen Elara**'s predecessor adopted these standards for royal wine purchases.

**International Distribution**: Wine required careful transport across Aethelgard's diverse climates and terrains. The consortium coordinated with the **Porters' and Teamsters' Guild** on temperature-controlled transport and the **Messengers' Guild** on vintage coordination.

### Modern Era (370-450 AS)

**Diplomatic Role**: The consortium's wines became essential to Aethelgard's diplomacy. **Queen Elara** serves consortium wines at state functions, and **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** provides elven vintages for elven-human diplomatic exchanges. Wine quality directly affects diplomatic outcomes.

**Southern Expanse Wines**: **Trader Slickscale** introduced **Southern Expanse** tropical wines to the consortium. **Lizardfolk** and tropical vintages expanded the consortium's product range significantly. **Chief Ssithik** authorized limited wine export from **Lizardfolk** vineyards.

**Brewers' Guild Coordination**: The consortium and **Brewers' Guild** coordinate on fermentation science and distribution networks. While occasionally competitive, the two organizations share transport infrastructure and quality standards.

## Organization and Structure

### Leadership Hierarchy

**Consortium Director**: Supreme authority, currently the human wine merchant **Vintage Copperwell III**, whose family has directed the consortium for generations. Director Copperwell coordinates with **Queen Elara** on diplomatic wines and **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** on elven vintages.

**Master Vintners**: Senior winemakers qualified to certify vintages and develop new varieties. Masters specialize in **Eastern Plains** wines, **Western Reaches** elven vintages, or **Southern Expanse** tropical wines.

**Journeyman Wine Merchants**: Qualified merchants authorized to distribute consortium wines. Journeymen operate wine shops and distribution networks across Aethelgard.

**Apprentice Vintners**: Students learning viticulture, winemaking, and wine commerce. Apprenticeships last 5-10 years, reflecting the long cycles of vineyard development and wine aging.

### Regional Divisions

**Eastern Plains Vineyards**: The consortium's largest production area, growing grapes in **Eastern Kingdoms**' temperate climate. **Poppy Greenfields**' soil management techniques improve vineyard yields.

**Western Reaches Elven Vineyards**: **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s elven vintages—the consortium's most prestigious products. Elven wines age for decades or centuries, producing flavors impossible to replicate.

**Southern Expanse Tropical Wines**: **Trader Slickscale** distributes **Lizardfolk** and tropical vintages through consortium channels. These unique wines expand the consortium's product range and market reach.

**Crystal Bay Distribution Hub**: **Mayor Ironscale**'s port serves as the consortium's primary distribution center, receiving wines from all regions and shipping across Aethelgard.

## Headquarters and Bases

The consortium headquarters in **Silverhaven** includes the **Grand Tasting Hall** where vintages are certified, the **Vintage Archive** storing reference bottles of every classified wine, and administrative offices coordinating international distribution. Regional offices in **Silvermoon** (elven vintages), the **Eastern Plains** (vineyard coordination), and **Crystal Bay** (distribution) manage local operations.

## Goals and Philosophy

### Wine Quality and Tradition

**Vintage Classification**: Every consortium wine receives a vintage classification based on grape quality, winemaking technique, and aging. **Queen Elara** and **King Thorin Stoneforge** both trust consortium classifications for royal purchases.

**Traditional Winemaking**: The consortium preserves traditional winemaking methods from each region. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s elven techniques, **Eastern Plains** human methods, and **Lizardfolk** tropical practices all receive equal respect.

**Innovation Within Tradition**: New grape varieties, aging techniques, and blending methods are encouraged but must pass quality evaluation. **Poppy Greenfields**' soil innovations produced new grape characteristics that earned consortium approval.

### International Distribution

**Climate-Controlled Transport**: Wine requires careful temperature management during transport. The consortium coordinates with the **Porters' and Teamsters' Guild** on insulated wagons and temperature-controlled storage.

**Diplomatic Wines**: The consortium selects wines for state functions based on the diplomatic context. **Queen Elara** serves **Eastern Plains** wines to human allies, elven vintages to **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s delegation, and **Dragonborn** wines to **Lady Zara Goldscale**.

**Market Access**: The consortium ensures wines reach every market in Aethelgard. **Trader Slickscale** handles **Southern Expanse** distribution; **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** manages urban wine shops.

## Membership and Recruitment

### Admission Requirements

**Palate Development**: Candidates must demonstrate refined taste discrimination and wine evaluation ability. The consortium tests candidates' ability to identify grape varieties, vintages, and regions blind.

**Viticulture Knowledge**: Understanding grape growing, harvest timing, and winemaking basics. **Poppy Greenfields** provides viticulture instruction for vineyard-focused members.

**Commercial Acumen**: Wine merchants must understand market dynamics, pricing, and distribution. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** evaluates candidates' business skills.

### Training Programs

**Viticulture Training**: Three to five years learning grape cultivation, harvest timing, and vineyard management. Apprentices work in consortium vineyards across all three wine regions.

**Winemaking Training**: Two to three years learning fermentation, aging, blending, and quality evaluation. Elven winemaking training occurs in **Silvermoon** under **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s supervision.

**Commerce Training**: One to two years learning distribution, pricing, and market dynamics. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** provides commercial instruction for wine merchants.

### Membership Demographics

**Humans** (40%): Vineyard owners and wine merchants dominating **Eastern Plains** production and urban distribution. Human members produce Aethelgard's most widely consumed wines.

**Elves** (20%): Elven vintners producing the consortium's most prestigious wines. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** restricts advanced elven winemaking to elven members.

**Lizardfolk** (15%): Tropical wine producers from the **Southern Expanse**. **Trader Slickscale** distributes their unique vintages through consortium channels.

**Halflings** (10%): Vineyard workers and cellar masters. **Poppy Greenfields**' **Halfling Agricultural Cooperative** provides vineyard management expertise.

**Other Species** (15%): **Catfolk** wine merchants distributing through **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s network, **dwarves** with underground aging caves, and **gnomes** with experimental fermentation techniques.

## Allies and Enemies

### Strategic Allies

**Queen Elara**: Primary patron serving consortium wines at all state functions. **Queen Elara**'s endorsement drives elite wine demand across **Eastern Kingdoms**.

**Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**: Elven patron providing the consortium's most prestigious vintages. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s elven wines command the highest prices and are essential for elven-human diplomacy.

**Trader Slickscale**: **Lizardfolk** trader distributing **Southern Expanse** wines through consortium channels. **Trader Slickscale** provides market access that the consortium could not achieve independently.

**Porters' and Teamsters' Guild**: Transport partner ensuring climate-controlled wine delivery across Aethelgard. Without reliable transport, the consortium could not distribute temperature-sensitive wines.

### Cautious Partners

**Brewers' Guild**: Fermentation colleagues who share some infrastructure and quality standards. Competition between wine and ale creates occasional tension, but the two organizations cooperate more than they compete.

**Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**: **Catfolk** trader distributing consortium wines through urban shops. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s commercial network expands reach but introduces profit pressure.

**Mayor Ironscale**: **Crystal Bay** patron hosting the consortium's primary distribution hub. **Mayor Ironscale** provides port facilities but expects favorable tax treatment in return.

### Ideological Opponents

**Wine Counterfeiters**: Criminals selling mislabeled or adulterated wines. The consortium investigates quality complaints and reports fraud. **Queen Elara** prosecutes wine fraud as a royal crime.

**Unauthorized Elven Wine Producers**: Vintners producing elven-style wines without **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s authorization. The consortium respects elven wine restrictions and reports violations.

**Shadowfen Wine Poisoners**: **Malakor Shadowdancer**'s agents seek to substitute poisoned wines at diplomatic functions. The consortium maintains strict security at all state functions where consortium wines are served.

## Notable Members

### Director Vintage Copperwell III

Human wine merchant whose family has directed the consortium for generations. **Copperwell** combines deep wine knowledge with diplomatic skill, selecting wines that smooth international relationships. **Queen Elara** relies on his diplomatic wine selections.

**Achievements**:
- Integrated **Southern Expanse** tropical wines into consortium distribution
- Established consortium wines as essential to Aethelgard's diplomatic functions
- Coordinated harvest timing across all three wine regions
- Negotiated **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s elven vintage sharing agreements
- Maintained vintage classification standards that **Queen Elara** trusts for royal purchases

### Master Silverleaf

**Elf** vintner managing **Silvermoon**'s elven wine production. Master Silverleaf produces the consortium's most prestigious vintages under **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s supervision, including century-aged wines served at state functions.

### Journeyman Tropicsun

**Lizardfolk** wine merchant managing **Southern Expanse** wine distribution through **Trader Slickscale**'s channels. Journeyman Tropicsun introduces Aethelgard to **Lizardfolk** tropical vintages that expand the consortium's product range.

## Notable Operations

### Diplomatic Wine Service (Ongoing)

The consortium selects and provides wines for all **Queen Elara**'s state functions. Wine selection is a diplomatic art—**Eastern Plains** wines for human allies, elven vintages for **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s delegation, **Dragonborn** wines for **Lady Zara Goldscale**. Director Copperwell personally selects each diplomatic wine.

### Elven Vintage Program (Ongoing)

**Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** provides limited quantities of elven vintages for consortium distribution. These century-aged wines command the highest prices in Aethelgard and are reserved for the most important occasions. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** ensures sustainable grape harvesting from **Western Reaches** vineyards.

### Southern Expanse Wine Introduction (435-448 AS)

**Trader Slickscale** and **Chief Ssithik** authorized limited **Lizardfolk** wine exports through consortium channels. The unique tropical vintages expanded the consortium's product range and market reach, introducing Aethelgard to entirely new flavor profiles.

### Queen Elara's Coronation Vintage (440 AS)

Director Copperwell personally selected and served the wines for **Queen Elara**'s coronation, including an elven vintage provided by **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** that had aged for 200 years. The coronation wines set the standard for diplomatic service across Aethelgard.

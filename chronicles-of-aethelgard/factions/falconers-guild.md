# The Falconers' Guild

## Overview

The Falconers' Guild trains birds of prey for hunting, messaging, and military reconnaissance across Aethelgard. Guild members work with **Eastern Kingdoms** royal mews, **Forest Wardens** for reconnaissance, and **Catfolk** information networks. The Guild maintains breeding programs and trains falconers from all species.

## Founding and History

The Falconers' Guild was founded in 360 AS, formalizing ancient traditions of working with birds of prey that predated written history across multiple species. Humans, elves, and even dwarves had maintained falconry practices for centuries, but the guild unified these traditions under a single organization.

### Founding Period (360-390 AS)

**Ancient Traditions Unified**: Human falconer **Bran Skysong** and elven bird-handler **Aerilon Wingshadow** jointly founded the guild to preserve and share falconry knowledge. **Bran Skysong** brought human hunting falconry, while **Aerilon Wingshadow** contributed elven communication-bird techniques.

**Royal Mews**: **King Aldric the Unifier** established the royal mews in **Silverhaven**, staffed by guild falconers. The royal mews became the guild's primary training facility and a symbol of official recognition. **Queen Elara** continues this tradition.

**Military Application**: **General Ironfist**'s predecessors recognized military reconnaissance applications. Trained hawks and falcons could observe enemy positions, deliver messages, and even attack small targets. The guild's military division was born.

### Modern Era (390-450 AS)

**Catfolk Partnership**: **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s **Catfolk** information network sought partnership with the guild for bird-based messaging. The collaboration produced the fastest communication network in Aethelgard, combining **Catfolk** urban routes with aerial messaging.

**Forest Wardens Alliance**: **Sylvaris Nightbreeze**'s **Forest Wardens** employ guild falconers for forest surveillance and wildlife monitoring. Birds of prey provide unmatched aerial reconnaissance of **Whispering Woods** and **Veilwood Forest**.

**Breeding Programs**: The guild established sophisticated breeding programs for birds of prey, selecting for intelligence, tameness, and specific abilities. The resulting lineages produce Aethelgard's finest hunting and messenger birds.

## Organization and Structure

### Leadership Hierarchy

**Guild Master**: Supreme authority, currently the human master falconer **Bran Skysong III**, descendant of the founding falconer. Guild Master Skysong maintains the guild's military partnerships and **Catfolk** messaging collaboration.

**Master Falconers**: Senior handlers qualified to train any species of bird of prey and evaluate breeding stock. Masters specialize in hunting, messaging, or military reconnaissance birds.

**Journeyman Falconers**: Qualified handlers authorized to work with common birds of prey independently. Journeymen staff the guild's civilian services—messaging, pest control, and recreational hunting.

**Apprentice Falconers**: Students learning bird handling, training, and care. Apprenticeships last 5-8 years, the longest of any guild due to the deep bond required between handler and bird.

### Divisions

**Royal Mews Division**: Elite falconers serving **Queen Elara**'s royal household, providing ceremonial displays, hunting parties, and secure messaging. **Queen Elara** personally sponsors this division.

**Military Reconnaissance Division**: Falconers attached to **General Ironfist**'s forces, training hawks for battlefield observation and message delivery. This division operates under military discipline during wartime.

**Forest Wardens Liaison**: Falconers working with **Sylvaris Nightbreeze**'s **Forest Wardens** for wildlife monitoring and forest surveillance. These specialists navigate dense woodland with their birds.

**Commercial Services Division**: Falconers providing messaging, pest control, and recreational hunting to the public. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** coordinates messaging services through his trading network.

## Headquarters and Bases

The guild headquarters in **Silverhaven** includes the **Royal Mews**, extensive aviaries, and training grounds. The headquarters' avian veterinary clinic is Aethelgard's finest. Regional aviaries operate in **Stonehold** (mountain birds), **Crystal Bay** (coastal raptors), and near **Whispering Woods** (forest species). Each facility breeds and trains birds suited to their environment.

## Goals and Philosophy

### Avian Partnership

**Mutual Respect**: The guild teaches that birds of prey are partners, not servants. Handlers who abuse or neglect birds face immediate expulsion. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze**'s **Forest Wardens** philosophy aligns with this approach.

**Breeding Excellence**: The guild maintains breeding programs that select for intelligence, health, and working ability. Each bird lineage is documented across generations, producing consistently excellent working birds.

**Species-Specific Training**: Different birds require different approaches. Eagles, hawks, falcons, and owls each have unique training requirements. The guild teaches specialists for each species rather than generic handlers.

### Service to Aethelgard

**Military Support**: Guild falconers provide reconnaissance and messaging for **General Ironfist**'s forces. Birds can observe enemy positions, deliver messages past blockades, and harass enemy logistics.

**Communication Network**: The guild operates Aethelgard's fastest messaging system outside magical communication. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s **Catfolk** network provides ground transport; guild birds provide aerial delivery.

**Environmental Monitoring**: **Forest Wardens** and **Beast Tamers' Guild** collaborate with the guild to monitor wildlife populations, track migration patterns, and detect environmental changes across Aethelgard.

## Membership and Recruitment

### Admission Requirements

**Patience and Empathy**: Handling birds of prey requires exceptional patience and genuine affection for avian partners. The guild evaluates candidates' temperament during extensive observation periods.

**Physical Fitness**: Falconry requires long hours outdoors in all weather, climbing to retrieve birds, and handling powerful raptors. The guild ensures candidates meet physical requirements.

**Long-Term Commitment**: Birds of prey bond with their handlers over years. The guild requires a minimum 15-year service commitment, ensuring birds have stable partners throughout their working lives.

### Training Programs

**Foundation Year**: Learning bird biology, behavior, and care basics. Apprentices work with experienced handlers, observing training techniques without handling birds directly.

**Handling Training**: Two to three years handling common birds under supervision. Apprentices learn perching, feeding, exercise, and basic commands. Each apprentice bonds with a training bird.

**Specialization**: Two to four years focusing on hunting, messaging, or military birds. Specialists learn advanced techniques for their chosen category. **General Ironfist**'s military staff provide reconnaissance training.

**Bird Bonding**: Each journeyman must bond with a personal bird that becomes their permanent partner. The guild matches handlers and birds based on temperament compatibility.

### Membership Demographics

**Humans** (40%): Most numerous falconers, versatile across all bird types and applications. Human guild members dominate the **Royal Mews** and commercial messaging services.

**Elves** (25%): Natural affinity for avian species, particularly forest raptors. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** trains elven falconers in **Whispering Woods** species. Elves excel at silent-messaging applications.

**Halflings** (15%): Patient handlers who form particularly strong bonds with smaller raptors. **Halfling** falconers excel at messaging due to their birds' maneuverability in urban environments.

**Other Species** (20%): **Catfolk** with their unique perspective on bird handling, **dwarves** specializing in mountain eagles, and **gnomes** developing miniature messenger birds.

## Allies and Enemies

### Strategic Allies

**Queen Elara**: Royal patron funding the **Royal Mews** and employing guild falconers for state communications and ceremonial occasions. **Queen Elara** considers the guild essential to royal operations.

**General Ironfist**: Military partner utilizing guild reconnaissance birds. **General Ironfist** relies on guild hawks for battlefield observation and message delivery during campaigns.

**Forest Wardens**: **Sylvaris Nightbreeze**'s **Forest Wardens** employ guild falconers for forest surveillance and wildlife monitoring. The partnership provides both reconnaissance capabilities and wildlife protection.

**Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**: **Catfolk** information broker coordinating aerial messaging with ground transport. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s network combined with guild birds creates Aethelgard's fastest communication system.

### Cautious Partners

**Messengers' Guild**: Potential competitor for messaging services. The two guilds cooperate through **Catfolk** intermediaries, but territorial disputes arise over messaging routes. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** mediates conflicts.

**Beast Tamers' Guild**: Overlapping expertise in animal handling. The falconers specialize in birds; the beast tamers in other creatures. Occasional disputes arise over which guild handles specific species.

**Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**: **High Elf** ruler permits limited elven falconry knowledge sharing but restricts certain **Whispering Woods** species to authorized handlers only.

### Ideological Opponents

**Bird Exploiters**: Black market traders capturing and selling wild birds illegally. The guild opposes all wild bird trade and reports poachers to **Forest Wardens** and royal authorities.

**Malakor Shadowdancer**: **Dark Elf** seeking trained birds for espionage operations. The guild refuses all **Shadowfen** orders and reports **Dark Elf** agents attempting to purchase birds.

## Notable Members

### Guild Master Bran Skysong III

Human master falconer maintaining his ancestor's vision of avian partnership. **Skysong** has bonded with three eagles over his career and personally trained **Queen Elara**'s personal hunting hawk. His military reconnaissance innovations have proven invaluable to **General Ironfist**.

**Achievements**:
- Established the **Catfolk** messaging partnership with **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**
- Developed military reconnaissance techniques adopted by **General Ironfist**'s forces
- Created the guild's breeding program for intelligence and tameness
- Trained 200+ falconers in multi-species bird handling
- Maintained the **Royal Mews** as Aethelgard's premier avian facility

### Master Wingshadow

**Elf** falconer specializing in forest raptors and **Whispering Woods** species. Master Wingshadow coordinates the **Forest Wardens** liaison and trains elven handlers in silent-messaging techniques.

### Journeyman Quickfeather

**Halfling** messaging specialist operating the fastest urban bird network in **Silverhaven**. Journeyman Quickfeather's small raptors navigate crowded streets that larger birds cannot manage, making them ideal for urban messaging.

## Notable Operations

### Royal Mews Service (Ongoing)

**Queen Elara**'s royal mews provide ceremonial birds for state occasions, hunting birds for royal parties, and secure messaging birds for official communications. The guild employs 30+ elite falconers in royal service.

### Military Reconnaissance Network (435-450 AS)

**General Ironfist** commissioned a permanent reconnaissance bird network for **Eastern Kingdoms** military operations. Guild falconers train hawks for battlefield observation, message delivery, and enemy position tracking. The network proved invaluable during **Northern Wastes** border incidents.

### Catfolk Messaging Partnership (430-450 AS)

**Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** and the guild established an integrated messaging network combining **Catfolk** ground routes with aerial bird delivery. The system provides the fastest non-magical communication across Aethelgard, with messages reaching any major city within hours.

### Forest Wardens Wildlife Survey (438-445 AS)

**Sylvaris Nightbreeze** commissioned the guild to survey wildlife populations in **Whispering Woods** and **Veilwood Forest** using trained raptors. The survey documented species populations, migration patterns, and environmental changes, providing crucial data for **Forest Wardens** conservation efforts.

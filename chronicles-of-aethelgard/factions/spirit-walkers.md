# Spirit Walkers

## Overview

The Spirit Walkers are an ancient **orcish** shamanic order dedicated to communing with ancestral spirits, nature spirits, and the elemental forces of Aethelgard. Based primarily in **Grommashar** and the orcish territories of the **Eastern Plains**, the order serves as spiritual guides, healers, and advisors to orcish chieftains. Led by High Shaman Gral Spiritseer, the Spirit Walkers maintain orcish traditions while adapting to the changing political landscape following the **Treaty of Silverhaven**.

## History and Founding

### Ancient Origins

**Pre-Sundering Era:**
- Orcish shamanism predates written history
- Spirit communion central to orcish identity
- Oral traditions preserved
- Nature-based spirituality
- Ancestral veneration

**First Spirit Walkers:**
- Legendary founder: Gral the First (pre-Sundering)
- Received spirit visions
- Established communion practices
- Taught healing arts
- United orcish tribes spiritually

### Age of Conflict (250-450 AS)

**War of Shattered Skies:**
- **Grommash Bloodaxe** initially rejected shamans
- Spirit Walkers warned of war
- Prophesied orcish defeat
- Exiled from **Grommashar** (350 AS)
- Continued serving scattered tribes

**Spiritual Resistance:**
- Maintained orcish culture
- Healed war wounded
- Preserved traditions
- Advised resistance leaders
- Awaited return

**Reconciliation (420 AS):**
- **Grommash Bloodaxe** aging
- Spirit visions convinced him
- Order recalled to **Grommashar**
- Official orcish religion
- **Shaman Gral Spiritseer** appointed

### Modern Era (450 AS - Present)

**Post-War Renewal:**
- **Treaty of Silverhaven** (450 AS) signed
- **Grommash Bloodaxe** reformed
- Spirit Walkers central to reform
- Traditional practices restored
- Young orcs trained

**Current Status:**
- Official orcish religious authority
- **Grommashar** central temple
- Multi-faith cooperation (**Order of the Silver Dawn**)
- Adapting to peace
- Growing international presence

## Doctrine and Beliefs

### Core Tenets

**The Orcish Spirit Path:**

1. **Ancestors**: The dead guide the living
2. **Nature**: All things have spirits
3. **Elements**: Earth, air, fire, water sacred
4. **Balance**: Harmony between all forces
5. **Vision**: Spirits speak through dreams
6. **Healing**: Restore balance when broken
7. **Community**: Tribe above individual
8. **Honor**: Live worthy of ancestors

### Theology

**Spirit World:**
- Physical and spirit worlds overlap
- Ancestors exist in spirit realm
- Nature spirits inhabit natural features
- Elemental spirits control forces
- Spirit walkers bridge worlds

**Ancestral Veneration:**
- Ancestors watch over descendants
- Can be consulted through rituals
- Offer food and respect
- Seek guidance in decisions
- Honor through worthy living

**Nature Spirits:**
- Animals have spirit guides
- Plants possess consciousness
- Landscapes have guardians
- Weather has elemental forces
- All deserve respect

**Elemental Forces:**
- **Earth**: Stability, strength, endurance
- **Air**: Freedom, change, communication
- **Fire**: Passion, transformation, purification
- **Water**: Healing, emotion, adaptation
- Balance all four

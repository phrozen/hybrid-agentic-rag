# The Carpenters' Guild

## Overview

The Carpenters' Guild works with wood from **Whispering Woods**, **Veilwood Forest**, and **Southern Expanse** tropical timber, creating furniture, ships, and structures. The Guild balances sustainable harvesting with **Forest Wardens** oversight, trains carpenters in multiple species' techniques, and supplies **Crystal Bay Naval Yard** and **Silverhaven** construction.

## Founding and History

The Carpenters' Guild was founded in 330 AS, emerging from informal cooperatives of woodworkers who recognized the need for sustainable forestry practices and standardized construction quality. The guild's founding coincided with growing concern over deforestation in the **Eastern Plains** and **Western Reaches**.

### Founding Period (330-365 AS)

**Sustainable Vision**: Human carpenter **Oakheart Timbersmith** and elven woodshaper **Greenweaver** jointly founded the guild after witnessing **Whispering Woods** logging cause ecological damage. They recruited **Forest Wardens** leader **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** as an early advisor.

**Royal Charters**: **King Aldric the Unifier** granted the guild charter to regulate forestry and construction. **King Thorin Stoneforge** recognized guild carpenters' role in dwarven surface construction, and **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** approved limited elven woodshaping knowledge sharing.

**Forest Wardens Partnership**: The guild's founding charter included a binding agreement with the **Forest Wardens** governing sustainable harvesting quotas. This partnership remains the guild's most important ecological commitment.

### Modern Era (365-450 AS)

**Naval Construction**: **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s fleet expansion created massive demand for ship-grade timber. The guild partnered with the **Shipwrights' Consortium** to supply the **Crystal Bay Naval Yard** with sustainable timber.

**Urban Expansion**: **Silverhaven**'s population boom required vast quantities of construction lumber. The guild coordinated with the **Architects' Guild** to ensure structural timber met building codes. **Queen Elara** subsidized sustainable forestry to maintain supply.

**Halfling Techniques**: **Poppy Greenfields** introduced **Halfling** agricultural building techniques, including efficient grain storage and barn construction. These methods influenced guild carpentry practices across the **Eastern Plains**.

## Organization and Structure

### Leadership Hierarchy

**Guild Master**: Supreme authority, currently the human master carpenter **Aldric Oakheart**, grandson of founder **Oakheart Timbersmith**. Guild Master Oakheart balances construction demands with sustainable forestry, maintaining the **Forest Wardens** partnership that his grandfather established.

**Master Carpenters**: Senior woodworkers qualified to design structures, furniture, and ships. Masters specialize in construction carpentry, furniture making, or shipbuilding, with cross-training encouraged.

**Journeyman Carpenters**: Qualified carpenters authorized for standard construction and furniture work. Journeymen operate workshops and construction crews across Aethelgard.

**Apprentice Carpenters**: Students learning woodworking techniques. Apprenticeships last 5-7 years, with mandatory rotation through construction, furniture, and shipbuilding specialties.

### Regional Workshops

**Silverhaven Workshop**: The guild's largest workshop, supplying **Eastern Kingdoms** construction and furniture needs. **Queen Elara**'s royal residences use guild carpentry exclusively.

**Crystal Bay Shipyard Workshop**: Dedicated to naval timber processing, this workshop supplies the **Shipwrights' Consortium** and **Crystal Bay Naval Yard**. **Admiral Hornbreaker** depends on its output.

**Stonehold Surface Workshop**: Despite dwarven preference for stone, surface structures require wood. **King Thorin Stoneforge** contracts the guild for all wooden construction in dwarven surface settlements.

**Silvermoon Elven Workshop**: **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** permits limited elven woodshaping instruction here. Living-wood techniques are taught to approved guild members only.

## Headquarters and Bases

The guild headquarters stands in **Silverhaven**'s artisan district, a showcase of woodworking techniques featuring elven living-wood beams, dwarven jointwork, and human practical construction. The building itself demonstrates the guild's cross-species philosophy. Regional workshops operate in **Crystal Bay** (naval), **Stonehold** (surface construction), and **Silvermoon** (elven techniques). Each workshop includes seasoning yards, timber storage, and training facilities.

## Goals and Philosophy

### Sustainable Forestry

**Forest Wardens Agreement**: The guild's founding commitment to sustainable harvesting remains paramount. Annual quotas set by the **Forest Wardens** govern timber extraction from **Whispering Woods** and **Veilwood Forest**. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** personally reviews the quotas.

**Replanting Mandate**: For every tree harvested, the guild plants two seedlings. Over 80 years, this mandate has expanded **Whispering Woods** coverage by 15%. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** praises the guild's commitment to forest health.

**Alternative Materials**: The guild researches non-wood construction materials to reduce forestry pressure. **Gnomish Engineers Guild** collaborations produce metal and stone alternatives for some applications.

### Construction Excellence

**Multi-Species Standards**: Carpentry must serve human, halfling, dwarven, and elven dimensions. Furniture and structures accommodate diverse body sizes and cultural preferences. **King Thorin Stoneforge** insists on dwarven-proportioned work in dwarven territories.

**Structural Safety**: The guild coordinates with the **Architects' Guild** on load-bearing standards, joining techniques, and weather resistance. **Queen Elara** mandates guild inspection for all public buildings.

**Traditional Preservation**: The guild preserves endangered woodworking traditions. Elven living-wood techniques, nearly lost after the **War of Shattered Skies**, survive because guild masters documented and taught them to approved members.

## Membership and Recruitment

### Admission Requirements

**Manual Skill**: Candidates must demonstrate basic woodworking aptitude, including measuring, cutting, and joining. The guild provides foundation training for those with raw talent.

**Physical Fitness**: Carpentry requires strength, stamina, and dexterity. The guild's physical requirements ensure members can handle the work safely.

**Ecological Commitment**: Members must pledge adherence to sustainable forestry practices. Violations result in expulsion. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** investigates complaints against guild members.

### Training Programs

**Foundation Apprenticeship**: Three years learning basic woodworking—measuring, cutting, joining, and finishing. Apprentices work in communal workshops under journeyman supervision.

**Specialization Training**: Two to four years focusing on construction, furniture, or shipbuilding. The **Shipwrights' Consortium** provides naval carpentry instruction; the **Architects' Guild** teaches structural techniques.

**Masterwork Examination**: Candidates design and build a significant piece—a building frame, furniture suite, or ship component—evaluated by three masters. **Queen Elara** occasionally attends **Silverhaven** examinations.

### Membership Demographics

**Humans** (40%): Versatile carpenters working across all specialties. Human guild members dominate **Silverhaven** and **Eastern Plains** construction.

**Dwarves** (15%): Surface construction specialists. While dwarves prefer stone, surface structures require wood. **King Thorin Stoneforge** sponsors dwarven members who learn surface woodworking.

**Elves** (15%): Woodshaping and living-wood specialists. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** restricts advanced elven techniques to approved members. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** trains **Forest Wardens**-approved elven carpenters.

**Halflings** (20%): Agricultural and residential building specialists. **Poppy Greenfields**' influence ensured **Halfling** techniques are represented in guild training.

**Other Species** (10%): **Gnomes** specializing in precision joinery, **Catfolk** in flexible furnishings, and **Lizardfolk** in swamp-wood construction techniques from the **Southern Expanse**.

## Allies and Enemies

### Strategic Allies

**Forest Wardens**: The guild's most important ally. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** and the **Forest Wardens** set sustainable harvesting quotas that the guild strictly follows. This partnership ensures long-term wood supply and forest health.

**Architects' Guild**: Close coordination on structural standards and construction projects. The **Architects' Guild** designs; the **Carpenters' Guild** builds. **Queen Elara** mandates guild collaboration on public works.

**Shipwrights' Consortium**: Naval timber supply partnership. The **Shipwrights' Consortium** depends on the **Carpenters' Guild** for properly seasoned ship-grade timber. **Admiral Hornbreaker** funds joint operations.

**Queen Elara**: Primary royal patron. **Queen Elara** subsidizes sustainable forestry and mandates guild carpentry for public buildings. The guild supplies all royal construction needs.

### Cautious Partners

**Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**: **High Elf** ruler permits limited elven technique sharing but restricts sacred living-wood methods. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** enforces these restrictions, creating tension with the guild's openness philosophy.

**King Thorin Stoneforge**: Dwarven patron for surface construction only. **King Thorin Stoneforge** values the guild's work but insists dwarven traditions prioritize stone. Underground construction remains dwarven-only domain.

**Halfling Agricultural Cooperative**: Partner in **Eastern Plains** construction. **Poppy Greenfields** ensures agricultural buildings don't interfere with farming. Land-use disputes require mediation.

### Ideological Opponents

**Illegal Loggers**: Unregulated timber extraction threatens sustainable forestry quotas. The guild reports illegal logging to the **Forest Wardens** and royal authorities. **Queen Elara** prosecutes convicted loggers.

**Clear-Cut Developers**: Construction interests seeking cheap timber without sustainability commitments. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** sometimes mediates disputes between developers and the guild.

**Shadowfen Timber Seekers**: **Malakor Shadowdancer**'s agents seek rare woods for shadow construction. The guild refuses all **Dark Elf** timber orders and reports suspicious purchase attempts.

## Notable Members

### Guild Master Aldric Oakheart

Human master carpenter and grandson of founder **Oakheart Timbersmith**. Aldric leads the guild with his grandfather's commitment to sustainable forestry, maintaining the **Forest Wardens** partnership that defines guild operations. **Queen Elara** considers him a trusted advisor on construction matters.

**Achievements**:
- Renegotiated the **Forest Wardens** sustainable harvesting agreement for another century
- Coordinated timber supply for **Crystal Bay Naval Yard** expansion
- Established **Halfling** agricultural building standards for the **Eastern Plains**
- Integrated elven living-wood techniques into guild training with **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s approval
- Oversaw **Silverhaven**'s residential district construction using sustainable timber

### Master Woodshaper Greenbranch

**Elf** carpenter specializing in living-wood construction. Master Greenbranch bridges elven tradition and guild practicality, training approved members in basic woodshaping techniques while respecting **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s restrictions.

### Journeyman Broadbeam

**Halfling** carpenter managing the **Eastern Plains** workshop. Journeyman Broadbeam implements **Poppy Greenfields**' agricultural building designs, developing efficient storage and barn structures for **Halfling** farming communities.

## Notable Operations

### Whispering Woods Sustainable Harvest (Ongoing)

The guild's flagship forestry program, harvesting timber from **Whispering Woods** under **Forest Wardens** supervision. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** personally approves all extraction zones. The program has maintained sustainable yields for over a century while expanding forest coverage.

### Crystal Bay Naval Timber Supply (435-450 AS)

**Admiral Hornbreaker** commissioned massive timber orders for naval construction. The guild coordinated with the **Shipwrights' Consortium** to deliver properly seasoned ship-grade timber without exceeding sustainable quotas. **Crystal Bay Naval Yard** construction remained on schedule throughout.

### Silverhaven Residential Construction (420-445 AS)

**Queen Elara** commissioned guild carpentry for **Silverhaven**'s expanding residential districts. The project required 15,000 timber units annually for 25 years. Guild Master Oakheart coordinated with the **Architects' Guild** and **Halfling Agricultural Cooperative** to minimize farmland impact.

### Eastern Plains Agricultural Building Program (432-442 AS)

**Poppy Greenfields** partnered with the guild to develop standardized agricultural buildings across the **Eastern Plains**. The program produced efficient grain storage, barn, and farmhouse designs that improved agricultural productivity throughout **Halfling** farming communities.

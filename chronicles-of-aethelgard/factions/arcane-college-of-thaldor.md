# Arcane College of Thaldor

## Overview

The Arcane College of Thaldor is the premier magical academy in Aethelgard, founded in 342 AS by the High Elf archmage **Throndor the Wise**. Located in the city of **Thaldor**, the College revolutionized magical education by admitting students of all species, standardizing magical theory across cultures, and integrating practical application with scholarly research. It remains the most prestigious magical institution in the world.

## Founding and History

### Vision and Foundation (342 AS)

**Throndor the Wise's** revolutionary idea:

**Traditional System:**
- Magic taught within species boundaries
- Elven magic separate from human magic
- Dwarven runemagic isolated
- Knowledge hoarded, not shared

**Throndor's Innovation:**
- Multi-species student body
- Standardized curriculum
- Eight schools of magic framework
- Knowledge shared across cultural lines
- Practical application emphasized

**Founding:**
- Land donated by human kingdoms
- **King Aldric the Unifier** supported initiative
- First students admitted (all species)
- **Throndor** served as first Archchancellor

### Early Years (342-400 AS)

**Growth Period:**
- Student body grew from 100 to 2,000
- Faculty recruited from all species
- Eight schools formalized
- Research towers constructed
- **Hall of Archmages** built (360 AS)

**Challenges:**
- Traditional elven academies opposed
- Dwarven secrecy concerns
- Human skepticism about elven magic
- Species tensions among students
- **Throndor's** leadership held it together

**Breakthroughs:**
- **Grimjaw Ironhand** collaboration (345 AS)
- Throndor-Ironhand runes developed
- Metal enchantment revolutionized
- Cross-species magical adaptation
- Reputation established

### War Period (400-450 AS)

**War Contributions:**
- Mages trained for defensive magic
- Research into military applications
- **Throndor** led defense of **Silverhaven** (445 AS)
- College served as hospital
- Proved value of magical education

**Siege of Silverhaven** (445 AS):
- **Throndor's** Mirror Ward held city
- College graduates fought in defense
- **General Sarah Ironside** integrated mages
- Magical-military coordination proven
- Enrollment surged after victory

### Age of Splendor (450 AS - Present)

**Expansion:**
- Student body: 10,000+ currently
- Faculty: 1,000+ from all species
- Campus expanded multiple times
- Branch campuses established
- International reputation solidified

**Current Leadership:**
- **Archchancellor Marcus Thornfield** (Human) - Third Archchancellor
- Faculty Council governs academic matters
- Board of Trustees oversees finances
- Alumni network influential across Aethelgard

### Eight Schools of Magic

The College organizes magical study into eight schools, as codified by **Throndor the Wise**:

**1. Abjuration**
- Focus: Protection, wards, defensive magic
- Head: **Professor Wardmaster Silverstone** (Dwarf)
- Notable: **Throndor's** specialty
- Applications: Personal protection, fortress wards

**2. Conjuration**
- Focus: Summoning, creation, teleportation
- Head: **Archmage Summoner Brightspell** (Human)
- Applications: Combat summons, transportation

**3. Divination**
- Focus: Scrying, prediction, knowledge
- Head: **Seer Moonshadow** (Elf)
- Notable: **Throndor's** secondary specialty
- Applications: Intelligence, prophecy

**4. Enchantment**
- Focus: Mind-affecting, charms, compulsions
- Head: **Professor Charmcaster** (Tiefling)
- Applications: Diplomacy, crowd control

**5. Evocation**
- Focus: Elemental energy, fire, lightning
- Head: **Archmage Fireborn** (Tiefling)
- Applications: Combat magic, energy projection

**6. Illusion**
- Focus: Deception, phantasms, invisibility
- Head: **Master Illusionist Whisperwind** (Gnome)
- Notable: Gnomish specialty
- Applications: Stealth, entertainment

**7. Necromancy**
- Focus: Death, undeath, life force
- Head: **Professor Gravekeeper** (Human)
- Applications: Healing, undead study (regulated)

**8. Transmutation**
- Focus: Transformation, alteration, polymorph
- Head: **Archmage Shapeshifter** (Dragonborn)
- Applications: Enhancement, material change

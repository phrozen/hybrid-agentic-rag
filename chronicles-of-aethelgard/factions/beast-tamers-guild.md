# The Beast Tamers' Guild

## Overview

The Beast Tamers' Guild trains handlers for magical creatures across Aethelgard, from **Dragonborn** dragon bonding to **Lizardfolk** swamp creature domestication. The Guild operates facilities near **Veilwood Forest**, **Whispering Woods**, and **Dragon Wastes**, teaching safe interaction with magical fauna. **Arcane College** researchers study creature intelligence through the Guild, and **Admiral Hornbreaker** employs tamers for naval mascots.

## Founding and History

The Beast Tamers' Guild was founded in 355 AS by a coalition of experienced handlers who recognized that Aethelgard's magical creatures posed both opportunities and dangers requiring specialized expertise. Untrained encounters with griffons, wyverns, and swamp beasts had caused casualties that organized training could prevent.

### Founding Period (355-385 AS)

**Early Handlers**: The guild's founders included the **Dragonborn** dragon-bonding master **Korralath Skysworn**, the **Lizardfolk** swamp handler **Hunter Zek**'s predecessor **Slithertongue**, and the human falconer **Bran Falconer**. Each brought species-specific creature knowledge that formed the guild's foundation.

**Royal Recognition**: **King Aldric the Unifier** granted the guild charter after handlers proved instrumental in defending frontier settlements against wild magical beast attacks. **Queen Elara** later expanded the charter to cover all of **Eastern Kingdoms**.

**Facility Establishment**: The guild built its first training compounds near **Veilwood Forest**, where diverse magical creatures provided real-world training environments. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** of the **Forest Wardens** negotiated access protocols for forest-adjacent facilities.

### Modern Era (385-450 AS)

**Scientific Partnership**: The **Arcane College of Thaldor** established a formal research relationship, with **Archchancellor Marcus Thornfield** funding creature intelligence studies. Guild handlers provide specimens and behavioral data; researchers publish findings benefiting both organizations.

**Naval Integration**: **Admiral Hornbreaker** recognized the value of trained creatures for naval reconnaissance and messaging. The guild now trains sea hawks, dolphins, and **Southern Expanse** water serpents for **Crystal Bay** naval operations.

**Dragon Wastes Outpost**: The guild established a permanent facility near the **Dragon Wastes** to study and manage dragon-creature interactions. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s **Dragonborn** tamers operate this dangerous but essential station.

## Organization and Structure

### Leadership Hierarchy

**Guild Master**: Supreme authority, currently the **Dragonborn** master tamer **Korralath Skysworn II**, grandson of the founding master. Guild Master Skysworn coordinates all guild operations and maintains relationships with royal patrons. **Lady Zara Goldscale** and **Queen Elara** both consult him on creature-related policy.

**Master Handlers**: Senior tamers qualified to train any species of magical creature. Masters specialize in creature categories—avian, reptilian, mammalian, or aquatic—and supervise training compounds.

**Journeyman Tamers**: Qualified handlers authorized to work with common magical creatures independently. Journeymen staff the guild's civilian services, from messenger birds to carriage draft beasts.

**Apprentice Handlers**: Students learning creature behavior, handling techniques, and safety protocols. Apprenticeships last 5-10 years depending on creature specialization.

### Training Compounds

**Veilwood Compound**: Primary training facility near **Veilwood Forest**, housing diverse magical creatures in naturalistic enclosures. The compound trains tamers for forest and plains creature handling. **Hunter Zek** frequently visits to share **Lizardfolk** techniques.

**Whispering Woods Station**: Specialized facility for avian and forest creature training, operated with **Forest Wardens** permission. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** limits access to protect sensitive forest species.

**Dragon Wastes Outpost**: Dangerous posting studying dragon-adjacent creatures and managing wild dragon interactions. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s **Dragonborn** tamers dominate this compound's staffing.

**Crystal Bay Marine Station**: Aquatic creature training facility serving **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s naval requirements. Marine tamers train sea hawks, dolphins, and water serpents for reconnaissance and messaging.

## Headquarters and Bases

The guild headquarters sits on the edge of **Veilwood Forest**, a sprawling compound of naturalistic enclosures, training arenas, and research buildings. The main hall features a living roof planted with native vegetation, attracting local creatures naturally. Satellite compounds operate near the **Whispering Woods**, **Dragon Wastes**, and **Crystal Bay**, each designed for their regional creature populations.

## Goals and Philosophy

### Harmonious Coexistence

**Respect for Creatures**: The guild teaches that magical creatures deserve respect and ethical treatment. Handlers must understand creature needs, psychology, and natural behaviors before attempting training. **Hunter Zek**'s **Lizardfolk** philosophy—that creatures are partners, not tools—permeates guild doctrine.

**Conservation Mandate**: The guild opposes capturing endangered species or removing creatures from sustainable habitats. **Chief Ssithik** and **Shaman Vessska** enforce this mandate for **Southern Expanse** creatures, while **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** protects forest species.

**Scientific Understanding**: Partnering with the **Arcane College of Thaldor** to document creature intelligence, behavior, and magical properties. **Archchancellor Marcus Thornfield** ensures research standards protect creature welfare.

### Safety First

**Handler Certification**: No tamer may work with dangerous creatures without master-level certification. The guild investigates all handling incidents and revokes certifications for negligence. **Queen Elara** requires guild certification for any magical creature kept within **Silverhaven**.

**Creature Registration**: All trained magical creatures are registered with the guild, including ownership, training level, and behavioral history. **General Ironfist**'s military creatures all carry guild documentation.

**Emergency Response**: The guild maintains rapid-response teams for rogue creature incidents. When a wyvern escaped near **Silverhaven**, guild handlers recaptured it within hours, preventing civilian casualties.

## Membership and Recruitment

### Admission Requirements

**Natural Affinity**: Candidates must demonstrate calm authority around creatures and intuitive understanding of animal behavior. **Dragonborn** candidates show innate dragon-bonding aptitude, while **Lizardfolk** candidates excel with swamp creatures.

**Physical Resilience**: Handling magical creatures requires strength, reflexes, and endurance. The guild's physical testing ensures candidates can survive creature encounters.

**Patience and Empathy**: The guild values handlers who form genuine bonds with creatures rather than dominating them. **Hunter Zek** personally evaluates candidates for this quality.

### Training Programs

**Foundation Year**: Apprentices study creature biology, behavior theory, and safety protocols. The **Arcane College** provides veterinary science instruction.

**Creature Specialization**: Apprentices choose one or two creature categories for intensive study. **Dragonborn** dragon-bonding requires the longest specialization—up to eight additional years.

**Field Certification**: Journeyman candidates must successfully train and handle a creature independently, demonstrating safe partnership. Three master handlers evaluate each certification attempt.

### Membership Demographics

**Humans** (30%): Versatile handlers working with diverse creatures across **Eastern Kingdoms**. Many serve as military and civilian messengers and handlers.

**Dragonborn** (20%): Innate dragon-bonding specialists. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s **Dragonborn** clan provides the guild's most specialized tamers. Every **Dragonborn** handler can bond with at least one dragon species.

**Lizardfolk** (15%): Swamp and reptilian creature experts. **Hunter Zek** and **Chief Ssithik**'s people provide unmatched expertise in **Southern Expanse** fauna. **Shaman Vessska** trains tamers in spiritual creature bonding.

**Elves** (15%): Forest creature specialists. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** trains elven handlers in **Whispering Woods** creature techniques. Wood elves demonstrate particular aptitude with avian species.

**Other Species** (20%): **Halflings** with domestic beasts, **Catfolk** with feline predators, **gnomes** with small magical creatures, and **minotaurs** with large mounts.

## Allies and Enemies

### Strategic Allies

**Arcane College of Thaldor**: Research partnership studying creature intelligence and magical properties. **Archchancellor Marcus Thornfield** funds guild researchers, and the college provides magical creature theory instruction.

**Admiral Hornbreaker**: Primary military contractor for naval creature programs. **Admiral Hornbreaker** funds the **Crystal Bay Marine Station** and employs guild-trained handlers aboard naval vessels.

**Lady Zara Goldscale**: **Dragonborn** patron supporting dragon-bonding programs. **Lady Zara Goldscale** provides funding and **Dragonborn** expertise for the **Dragon Wastes Outpost**.

**Hunter Zek**: **Lizardfolk** master handler and guild advisor. **Hunter Zek** bridges the guild and **Chief Ssithik**'s people, ensuring ethical **Southern Expanse** operations.

### Cautious Partners

**Chief Ssithik**: **Lizardfolk** leader permits guild access to **Southern Expanse** creatures with strict quotas. **Hunter Zek** and **Shaman Vessska** negotiate annual access agreements.

**Forest Wardens**: **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** restricts guild operations in **Whispering Woods** to protect sensitive species. The guild respects these limits while advocating for expanded research access.

**Queen Elara**: Royal patron who regulates magical creature ownership in **Eastern Kingdoms**. **Queen Elara** occasionally restricts creature imports for public safety, limiting guild operations.

### Ideological Opponents

**Creature Exploiters**: Black market handlers who capture and abuse magical creatures for profit. The guild hunts these criminals relentlessly. **Queen Elara** prosecutes creature traffickers on guild recommendation.

**Shadowfen Collective**: **Malakor Shadowdancer** seeks trained creatures for shadow operations, particularly intelligent species for infiltration. The guild refuses all orders from **Dark Elf** intermediaries.

**Rogue Handlers**: Former guild members expelled for abuse or negligence who continue working illegally. **General Ironfist** assists in apprehending these dangerous individuals.

## Notable Members

### Guild Master Korralath Skysworn II

**Dragonborn** master handler and dragon-bonding specialist. Korralath leads the guild with the same fire as his grandfather, maintaining cross-species cooperation while advancing **Dragonborn** traditional techniques. **Lady Zara Goldscale** considers him her most trusted creature advisor.

**Achievements**:
- Established the **Dragon Wastes Outpost** for dragon-creature management
- Negotiated **Chief Ssithik**'s creature access agreements
- Trained **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s naval messenger hawk fleet
- Integrated **Lizardfolk** swamp handling techniques into guild training
- Maintained zero handler fatalities at the **Veilwood Compound** for 15 years

### Master Handler Vessska Scalefriend

**Lizardfolk** handler trained by **Shaman Vessska** in spiritual creature bonding. Vessska Scalefriend trains all **Southern Expanse** creature specialists and serves as liaison to **Chief Ssithik**'s people.

### Journeyman Bran Featherlight

Human handler specializing in avian creatures. Bran manages the **Crystal Bay Marine Station**'s sea hawk program and trains **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s naval messenger birds.

## Notable Operations

### Dragon Wastes Settlement (442-450 AS)

The guild established a permanent outpost near the **Dragon Wastes** to study dragon-adjacent creatures and manage wild dragon encounters. **Lady Zara Goldscale** provided **Dragonborn** handlers, while **Korralath Skysworn II** personally oversaw construction. The outpost prevents dragon-creature incursions into settled areas.

### Naval Creature Program (435-450 AS)

**Admiral Hornbreaker** commissioned the guild to develop trained sea creatures for **Crystal Bay** naval operations. The program trains sea hawks for ship-to-ship messaging, dolphins for underwater reconnaissance, and water serpents for harbor defense. The **Crystal Bay Marine Station** now houses over 200 trained naval creatures.

### Veilwood Creature Census (440-445 AS)

The guild conducted a comprehensive census of magical creatures in **Veilwood Forest**, documenting species populations, migration patterns, and habitat health. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** and the **Forest Wardens** cooperated fully. The census data informs conservation policies across Aethelgard.

### Emergency Creature Response (447 AS)

When a wild wyvern terrorized **Silverhaven**'s outskirts, guild handlers responded within hours. Master Handler Vessska Scalefriend led the recapture team, subduing the creature without injury to handler or beast. **Queen Elara** cited the guild's rapid response as evidence for increased royal funding.

## Expansion Guidelines

When creating faction entries, follow these principles:

1. **Use the Faction Template** from AGENTS.md
2. **Include 3-5 cross-references** to characters, locations, events, or other factions
3. **Match filename to H1** (e.g., `arcane-college-of-thaldor.md` → `# Arcane College of Thaldor`)
4. **Describe organization, goals, and membership**
5. **Connect to historical events** and notable members

### Faction Density Targets

- **Phase 4**: Major factions (15-20 total)
- **Phase 5**: Regional and specialized factions (30-40 total)
- **Phase 6**: Minor factions and fill gaps (50+ total)

## Faction Relationships

### Known Alliances
- **Arcane College** collaborates with multiple racial institutions
- **Order of the Silver Dawn** works with **Aasimar** communities
- **Dwarven-Human Trade Alliance** from Treaty of Silverhaven

### Known Rivalries
- **Dark Elves** vs. **Deep Dwarves** - Underground territorial disputes
- **Elves** vs. **Dwarves** - Historical resource conflicts (improving)
- Various factions during **War of Shattered Skies**

### Neutral Organizations
- **Halfling Inns** - Granted neutral status by Hospitality Accords
- **Changeling Diplomatic Corps** - Mediators and information brokers
- **Catfolk Merchant Networks** - Trade over politics

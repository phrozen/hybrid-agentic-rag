# Order of the Sacred Anvil

## Overview

The Order of the Sacred Anvil is the primary religious order of the dwarven people, dedicated to the worship of Moradin the All-Father, creator god of the dwarven pantheon. Based in **Stonehold**'s Temple of the Sacred Anvil, the order serves as spiritual guides, oath-keepers, and guardians of dwarven traditions. Led by High Priest Thrain Sacredforge, the order plays a crucial role in dwarven society, sanctifying births, marriages, deaths, and oaths while maintaining the spiritual connection between dwarves and their divine creator.

## History and Founding

### Ancient Origins

**Pre-Sundering Era:**
- Dwarven faith predates written history
- Moradin worship central to dwarven identity
- First temples built in ancient holds
- Oral traditions preserved
- Runemagic integrated with faith

**The First Anvil:**
- Legendary artifact from Moradin himself
- Given to first dwarven priests
- Used to forge first dwarven weapons
- Symbol of divine favor
- Location unknown (possibly in **Stonehold** depths)

- Temple of the Sacred Anvil founded (100 AS)

- Priesthood organized

**Order Formalization:**
- Monastic rules codified
- Priest hierarchy established
- Training programs developed
- Mission assignments began
- Oath ceremonies standardized

### Age of Conflict (250-450 AS)

### Modern Era (450 AS - Present)

**Post-War Renewal:**

## Doctrine and Beliefs

### Core Tenets

**The Dwarven Creed:**

1. **Creation**: Moradin forged dwarves from stone and metal
2. **Purpose**: Dwarves must create, build, and improve
3. **Community**: Clan and hold above individual
4. **Oaths**: Promises are sacred and binding
5. **Craft**: Excellence in work is worship
6. **Courage**: Face challenges with determination
7. **Tradition**: Honor ancestors and preserve culture
8. **Stone**: Mountain is sacred, earth is blessed

### Theology

**Moradin the All-Father:**
- Creator god of dwarves
- God of creation, crafting, and family
- Depicted as mighty dwarf with hammer
- Forged dwarves in cosmic forge
- Watches over dwarven souls

**Divine Pantheon:**
- **Moradin**: Chief deity, creator
- **Berronar**: Wife, goddess of hearth and home
- **Dumathoin**: Keeper of secrets under stone
- **Thard Harr**: Wilderness and survival
- **Abbathor**: Greed and wealth (cautious worship)
- **Clangeddin Silverbeard**: War and battle

**Afterlife Beliefs:**
- Virtuous dwarves join Moradin in celestial forge
- Continue crafting in afterlife
- Ancestors watch over living
- Can be consulted through rituals
- Reincarnation within clan (some beliefs)

### Sacred Texts

**The Stone Tablets:**
- Ancient religious texts
- Carved in stone (literal and metaphorical)
- Moradin's teachings
- Clan histories
- Ritual instructions

**The Forge Hymns:**
- Sacred songs and prayers
- Sung during crafting
- Blessing ceremonies
- Battle chants
- Funeral dirges

**The Oath Book:**
- Recorded oaths and vows
- Binding agreements
- Historical promises
- Legal-religious hybrid
- Priesthood maintains

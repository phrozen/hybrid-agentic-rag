# The Everflame Peak

## Overview

Everflame Peak is an active volcano in the **Southern Expanse** volcanic zone, sacred to **Fire Genasi** and home to the legendary **Flameforge** city. The volcano's central caldera contains an eternal flame that has burned for over a thousand years, believed to be a fragment of the elemental plane of Fire. **Pyre the Unquenchable**, the greatest Fire Genasi smith, maintains a forge in the eternal flame, crafting weapons and artifacts of legendary power. The peak is both a pilgrimage site and a strategic resource for volcanic materials.

## Geography and Climate

Everflame Peak rises 12,000 feet above the **Southern Expanse** desert, its volcanic cone visible for hundreds of miles. The volcano is active but stable, with regular lava flows that **Fire Genasi** channel into forges and workshops. The climate is extremely hot near the caldera, with temperatures exceeding 200°F, but cooler slopes support hardy vegetation.

### Volcanic Features

**Eternal Flame**: Central caldera contains magical fire burning for 1,000+ years. **Fire Genasi** believe it connects to the elemental plane of Fire.

**Lava Flows**: Controlled channels direct molten rock to **Flameforge** city forges. **Pyre the Unquenchable** regulates flow rates.

**Obsidian Cliffs**: Volcanic glass formations harvested by **Dragonborn** craftsmen from **Drakkenheim** and **Sunscorch**.

**Ash Fields**: Fertile volcanic soil supporting heat-adapted agriculture. **Halfling** farmers introduced crop varieties.

## History and Founding

Everflame Peak was discovered by **Fire Genasi** explorers in 245 AS, who recognized the eternal flame's magical significance. The **Flameforge** city was constructed over subsequent centuries, becoming a pilgrimage site and crafting center.

### Discovery Period (245-300 AS)

**First Pilgrims**: **Fire Genasi** clans established shrines around the eternal flame

**Flameforge Construction**: **Fire Genasi** smiths built forges channeling lava and eternal flame

**Pyre's Arrival**: **Pyre the Unquenchable** came to Everflame Peak in 298 AS, revolutionizing volcanic crafting

### Modern Era (300-450 AS)

**Dragonborn Alliance**: **Lady Zara Goldscale** formalized trade agreements with Everflame Peak

**Sunscorch Partnership**: Desert city became primary trade hub for Everflame Peak goods

**International Recognition**: **Queen Elara** and **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** acknowledge Everflame Peak's strategic importance

## Population and Culture

Everflame Peak's **Flameforge** city houses 5,000 residents, predominantly **Fire Genasi** (75%), **Dragonborn** craftsmen (15%), **Human** merchants (5%), and other species (5%). The city celebrates **Fire Genasi** elemental traditions while welcoming trading partners.

### Cultural Practices

**Flame Dancing**: **Fire Genasi** ritual combining martial arts and fire manipulation. Pilgrims perform flame dances before the eternal flame.

**Forge Blessings**: **Pyre the Unquenchable** blesses new forges annually, channeling eternal flame into them.

**Heat Trials**: Young **Fire Genasi** prove endurance by working forges bare-handed. Success grants adult status.

### Spiritual Significance

**Fire Genasi** believe the eternal flame is a fragment of their elemental homeland. Pilgrims travel from **Elemental Confluence**, **Crystal Bay**, and **Silverhaven** to worship at the flame.

## Economy and Trade

Everflame Peak's economy centers on volcanic crafting, pilgrimage services, and exotic materials:

### Volcanic Crafting

**Pyre's Workshop**: **Pyre the Unquenchable** crafts legendary fire-enchanted weapons. His blades are sought by heroes across Aethelgard, including **Queen Elara**'s champions.

**Dragonborn Collaboration**: **Dragonborn** smiths from **Drakkenheim** and **Sunscorch** work alongside **Fire Genasi**, learning volcanic techniques.

**Trade Goods**:
- **Fire-Enchanted Weapons**: Blades that burn without fuel
- **Heat-Resistant Armor**: Plate mail wearable in lava
- **Obsidian Artwork**: Volcanic glass sculptures
- **Flame Crystals**: Eternal flame fragments for magical items

### Pilgrimage Economy

**Shrine Donations**: Pilgrims contribute gold, materials, and labor to maintain Everflame Peak.

**Guided Tours**: **Fire Genasi** guides lead safe tours of volcanic features. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s tour companies book months in advance.

**Hospitality**: Inns and restaurants serve pilgrims, many operated by **Halfling** entrepreneurs.

## Notable Features

### The Eternal Flame

Central caldera's magical fire, burning for 1,000+ years. **Pyre the Unquenchable** maintains the flame, performing rituals to keep it stable. **Arcane College of Thaldor** researchers study the flame's planar connections.

### Flameforge City

**Fire Genasi** metropolis built into volcanic slopes. The city's forges channel lava flows, and buildings are carved from obsidian and basalt. **Pyre**'s workshop dominates the city center.

### Pilgrim Shrines

Multiple shrines around the caldera where pilgrims worship. The largest shrine, built by **Dragonborn** artisans, features scale-mail decorations honoring the eternal flame.

### Lava Channels

Engineered pathways directing molten rock to forges. **Fire Genasi** engineers maintain channels, preventing dangerous overflow. **General Ironfist** consulted on defensive channel designs.

## Governance and Leadership

### Pyre the Unquenchable

**Pyre** rules Everflame Peak as both spiritual leader and crafting master. His decisions on volcanic crafting and pilgrimage matters are final. **Pyre** advises **Lady Zara Goldscale** on **Dragonborn**-**Fire Genasi** relations.

### Fire Genasi Council

Elders from major **Fire Genasi** clans advise **Pyre** on community matters. The council includes representatives from **Elemental Confluence**, **Crystal Bay**, and **Sunscorch**.

### Trade Ministers

**Dragonborn** and **Human** merchants hold advisory positions, ensuring trade policies benefit all parties. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** maintains a permanent trade minister at Everflame Peak.

## Connected Characters

- **Pyre the Unquenchable**: **Fire Genasi** master smith, rules Everflame Peak
- **Lady Zara Goldscale**: **Dragonborn** leader, maintains trade alliance
- **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**: **Catfolk** trader, operates tour companies
- **General Ironfist**: Consulted on volcanic defense systems

## Connected Locations

- **Southern Expanse**: Desert region containing Everflame Peak
- **Flameforge City**: **Fire Genasi** metropolis built into volcano slopes
- **Sunscorch**: **Dragonborn** desert city, primary trade partner
- **Drakkenheim**: **Dragonborn** capital, collaborates on volcanic crafting
- **Elemental Confluence**: **Genasi** capital, spiritual connection to Everflame Peak

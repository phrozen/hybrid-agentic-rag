# Landmarks of Aethelgard

This index catalogs notable sites, ruins, monuments, natural wonders, and magical locations that hold significance in the world of Aethelgard.

---

## Magical Sites

### The Arcane College of Thaldor
- **Type**: Magical institution
- **Location**: Thaldor
- **Founded**: 342 AS by Throndor the Wise
- **Significance**: Premier magical academy, multi-species student body

### The Hall of Archmages
- **Type**: Museum and ceremonial hall
- **Location**: Within Arcane College of Thaldor
- **Significance**: Preserves legacy of great mages, displays Throndor's staff and robes

### The Radiant Temple
- **Type**: Religious site
- **Location**: Silverhaven
- **Significance**: Aasimar spiritual center, multi-faith acceptance

### The Planar Gateway
- **Type**: Research facility
- **Location**: Infernia (Tiefling sanctuary)
- **Significance**: Tiefling planar studies, magical research

# The Shattered Isles

## Overview

The Shattered Isles are a fragmented archipelago located off the southern coast of the **Southern Expanse**, formed during **the Sundering** when a single large landmass was broken apart by the cataclysmic magical and geological forces. The islands are known for their dangerous wild magic zones, exotic creatures, ancient ruins, and valuable resources, making them a haven for adventurers, pirates, researchers from the **Arcane College of Thaldor**, and those seeking to escape conventional society.

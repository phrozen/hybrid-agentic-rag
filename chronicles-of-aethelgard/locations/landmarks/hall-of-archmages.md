# The Hall of Archmages

## Overview

The Hall of Archmages is a prestigious museum and ceremonial hall located within the **Arcane College of Thaldor**, dedicated to preserving the legacy of Aethelgard's greatest mages throughout history. Founded by **Throndor the Wise** in 350 AS, the Hall houses artifacts, spellbooks, robes, and personal effects from deceased archmages, serving as both a memorial to magical excellence and an inspiration for current students. The Hall is curated by **Archchancellor Marcus Thornfield** and remains one of the most sacred sites in the magical community.

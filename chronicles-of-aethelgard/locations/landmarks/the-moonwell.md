# The Moonwell

## Overview

The Moonwell is a sacred pool of magical water located in the heart of **Silvermoon Forest** in the **Western Reaches**. Created during the elven exodus following **the Sundering**, the Moonwell serves as the spiritual and magical center of elven civilization, its waters possessing potent healing and divinatory properties. The well is guarded by the **High Elf Council of Silvermoon** and accessed only through rituals conducted by **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** herself or designated priestesses.

## Location and Access

**Geographic Position:**
- **Region**: **Silvermoon Forest**, **Western Reaches**
- **Coordinates**: Central forest, hidden grove
- **Distance**: 5 miles from **Silvermoon** city
- **Terrain**: Dense woodland, magical wards
- **Access**: Restricted, ritual entry only

**Security Measures:**
- Magical wards (ancient elven magic)
- Physical barriers (dense forest)
- Guardian spirits (nature elementals)
- **Forest Wardens** patrol perimeter
- **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** controls access

## Expansion Guidelines

When creating location entries, follow these principles:

1. **Use the Location Template** from AGENTS.md
2. **Include 3-5 cross-references** to characters, events, factions, or other locations
3. **Match filename to H1** (e.g., `silverhaven.md` → `# Silverhaven`)
4. **Describe geography, history, and culture**
5. **Connect to historical events** that occurred there

# Stormpeak Mountains

## Overview

The Stormpeak Mountains are a massive mountain range in central Aethelgard, forming the natural border between the Eastern Kingdoms and Western Reaches. Home to the greatest dwarven holds including **Stonehold**, the Stormpeaks are renowned for their mineral wealth, formidable peaks, and strategic importance. The range's name derives from the frequent thunderstorms that batter its heights, creating dramatic weather and dangerous conditions for travelers.

## Geography and Climate

### Location and Extent

**Geographic Position:**
- **Latitude**: Central Aethelgard (35°-45° N equivalent)
- **Longitude**: Eastern Kingdoms western border
- **Length**: Approximately 800 miles north-south
- **Width**: 100-200 miles east-west
- **Area**: Approximately 120,000 square miles

**Borders:**
- **North**: Northern Wastes transition
- **South**: Eastern Plains foothills
- **East**: Eastern Kingdoms (human territories)
- **West**: Western Reaches (elven forests)

### Major Peaks

**Highest Summits:**

1. **Thunderpeak** (18,000 feet)
   - Highest peak in Stormpeaks
   - Named for constant thunderstorms
   - **King Thorin Stoneforge** ceremonial site
   - Dangerous climbing conditions
   - Strategic observation point

2. **Ironspire** (16,500 feet)
   - **Stonehold** location
   - Dwarven capital mountain
   - Heavily fortified
   - Mining operations extensive
   - **Grimjaw Ironhand's** legacy

3. **Stonefang Peak** (15,800 feet)
   - Eastern range high point
   - Human kingdom border marker
   - Military installations
   - Trade route oversight
   - **Queen Elara** visits occasionally

4. **Silverhorn** (15,200 feet)
   - Western range prominence
   - Elven border territory
   - **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** diplomatic meetings
   - Scenic beauty
   - Limited access

5. **Dragon's Tooth** (14,900 feet)
   - Distinctive spire formation
   - Ancient dragon legends
   - **Dragonborn** pilgrimage site
   - Dangerous climbing
   - Mythical significance

### Passes and Routes

**Major Mountain Passes:**

1. **Thunder Pass** (8,500 feet)
   - Primary east-west route
   - **King Thorin Stoneforge** fortified
   - Trade route critical
   - Military control point
   - Year-round operation (difficult winter)

2. **Iron Gate** (7,200 feet)
   - **Stonehold** main entrance
   - Dwarven customs checkpoint
   - Heavily guarded
   - Trade processing
   - **Grimjaw Ironhand** constructed

3. **Eagle's Perch** (9,100 feet)
   - High-altitude route
   - Emergency passage only
   - Dangerous conditions
   - Military use primarily
   - Scenic but treacherous

4. **Merchant's Way** (6,800 feet)
   - Southern trade route
   - **Catfolk** caravans use
   - Gentler grades
   - Year-round operation
   - **Southern Expanse** connection

### Climate

**Alpine Climate Characteristics:**

**Temperature:**
- **Summer** (Months 4-6): 40-60°F at peaks, 60-80°F in valleys
- **Winter** (Months 10-12): -20 to 20°F at peaks, 20-40°F in valleys
- **Spring/Autumn**: Highly variable, rapid changes

**Precipitation:**
- **Annual**: 40-80 inches (varies by elevation)
- **Snow**: Heavy, 200-400 inches annually at high elevations
- **Rain**: Summer thunderstorms common
- **Thunderstorms**: Frequent (range name source)

**Weather Patterns:**
- Rapid weather changes
- Severe thunderstorms (summer)
- Heavy snowfall (winter)
- High winds (year-round)
- Fog and low visibility common

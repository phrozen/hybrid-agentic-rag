# Southern Expanse

## Overview

The Southern Expanse is a vast tropical and subtropical region in southern Aethelgard, home to diverse species including Dragonborn, Lizardfolk, and various tropical peoples. Known for its hot climate, extensive coastlines, swamp territories, and unique cultures, the Southern Expanse has become increasingly integrated into Aethelgard's trade networks since the **Treaty of Silverhaven**.

## Geography and Climate

### Location and Boundaries

The Southern Expanse occupies the southern portion of the main continent:

- **North**: Borders Eastern Kingdoms and Western Reaches
- **South**: Southern Sea and tropical islands
- **East**: Eastern Ocean coast
- **West**: Western Ocean coast

Total area: Approximately 2.5 million square miles

### Physical Features

**Coastlines:**
- **Crystal Bay**: Major trade hub, connects to **Silverhaven**
- **Dragon Coast**: Dragonborn settlements
- **Lizard Shore**: Lizardfolk coastal territories
- **Merchant's Bay**: Trade port region
- Extensive beaches and natural harbors

**Swamplands:**
- **The Bogs**: Vast wetlands, **Lizardfolk** heartland
- **Ssithik's Landing**: Swamp capital region
- **Sunken City**: Ancient ruins, partially submerged
- River deltas and marshes
- Unique swamp ecosystems

**Mountain Ranges:**
- **Dragonspine Peaks**: Central mountain chain
- **Coastal Range**: Eastern seaboard mountains
- **Volcanic Zone**: Active volcanoes (Fire Genasi territory)
- Mineral-rich highlands

**Rivers and Lakes:**
- **River Aethel**: Source in southern mountains, flows to **Silverhaven**
- **Plains River**: Waters orcish territories
- **Tropical River System**: Extensive waterways
- **Lake of Stars**: High-altitude lake, magical properties

### Climate

The Southern Expanse has tropical and subtropical climates:

- **Wet Season** (Months 1-6): Heavy rainfall, high humidity, lush growth
- **Dry Season** (Months 7-12): Less rain, warm temperatures, harvest time
- **Coastal Regions**: Moderate temperatures year-round
- **Interior**: Hot and humid
- **Mountains**: Cooler, temperate at high elevations

The climate supports diverse ecosystems from tropical rainforests to coastal mangroves to mountain cloud forests.

## History and Founding

### Ancient History (Pre-Sundering)

**Early Inhabitants:**
- **Lizardfolk** civilizations (oldest continuous culture)
- Swamp settlements and fishing communities
- Tribal organization
- Nature-based spirituality
- Limited outside contact

**Dragonborn Arrival:**
- Dragonborn clans arrived (pre-Sundering, exact date unknown)
- Fleeing draconic conflicts in north
- Established coastal settlements
- **Drakkenheim** founded
- Maritime culture developed

### Age of Expansion (50-250 AS)

**Trade Development:**
- **Catfolk** trade networks established (390 AS)
- Coastal trading posts
- Exotic goods to northern kingdoms
- **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** expanded networks
- Economic integration began

**Dragonborn Unification:**
- **Dragonborn** clans unified (370 AS)
- **Lord Arkhan the Cruel** unified coastal clans
- **Drakkenheim** established as capital
- Naval power developed
- Trade agreements negotiated

### Age of Shattered Skies (250-450 AS)

**Relative Isolation:**
- Less affected by northern war
- Trade continued (profitable neutrality)
- **Catfolk** maintained networks
- Some mercenary service (Dragonborn)
- Refugees accepted

**Naval Development:**
- **Minotaur** naval cooperation (365 AS)
- **Admiral Hornbreaker** charted southern routes
- Trade lane protection
- Pirate suppression
- Maritime law established

### Age of Splendor (450 AS - Present)

**Integration:**
- **Treaty of Silverhaven** (450 AS) signed
- Trade expanded dramatically
- **Dragonborn** joined coalition
- **Lizardfolk** trade agreements (430 AS)
- Cultural exchange increased

**Current Status:**
- Major trade partner with Eastern Kingdoms
- Exotic goods supplier
- Naval power in southern seas
- Growing tourism
- Species diversity increasing

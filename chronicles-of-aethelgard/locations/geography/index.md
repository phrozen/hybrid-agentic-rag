# Geography of Aethelgard

This index catalogs the continents, regions, mountain ranges, forests, rivers, and other geographical features that make up the physical world of Aethelgard.

---

## Continents

### Eastern Kingdoms
- **Description**: Human-dominated lands with diverse multi-species cities
- **Notable Features**: Silverhaven, Eastern Plains, Crystal Bay
- **Dominant Species**: Humans, with all major races represented

### Western Reaches
- **Description**: Ancient elven forests and dwarven mountain holds
- **Notable Features**: Silvermoon forest, dwarven mountain ranges
- **Dominant Species**: Elves, Dwarves

### Southern Expanse
- **Description**: Tropical regions home to lizardfolk and dragonborn
- **Notable Features**: Swamps, coastal regions, dragonborn territories
- **Dominant Species**: Lizardfolk, Dragonborn

### Northern Wastes
- **Description**: Harsh tundra and frozen mountains
- **Notable Features**: Frozen peaks, arctic plains
- **Dominant Species**: Hardly settled, some barbarian human tribes

### The Shattered Isles
- **Description**: Archipelago of mysterious islands
- **Notable Features**: Unknown, largely unexplored
- **Dominant Species**: Varied, isolated communities

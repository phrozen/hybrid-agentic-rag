# Infernia

## Overview

Infernia is a **tiefling** sanctuary city located in the **Shattered Isles**, specifically on **Refuge Rock**, established during **The Great Persecution** as a haven for planar-touched refugees. The city maintains strong planar connections through the **Planar Gateway** research facility, with **tiefling** scholars studying their infernal heritage. The **Tiefling Refugee Network** operates safe houses in Infernia, and **Malakor Shadowdancer** provides funding through the **Shadowfen Collective**.

## Geography and Climate

Infernia sits atop **Refuge Rock**, a volcanic island in the **Shattered Isles** archipelago. The island's volcanic activity creates natural planar weak points, allowing **tieflings** to sense their infernal heritage more strongly. The climate is warm and dry, with volcanic soil supporting unique flora adapted to planar energies.

### Refuge Rock Features

- **Volcanic Peaks**: Three dormant volcanoes surrounding the city
- **Planar Rifts**: Natural weak points in reality, studied by scholars
- **Obsidian Cliffs**: Black stone cliffs providing natural defense
- **Ash Fields**: Fertile volcanic soil for agriculture

## History and Founding

Infernia was founded in 385 AS during **The Great Persecution**, when **tieflings** faced widespread discrimination across Aethelgard. The first refugees discovered **Refuge Rock**'s planar connections and established a sanctuary city.

### Founding Period (385-400 AS)

**First Refuge**: **Tiefling** families fleeing persecution found sanctuary on **Refuge Rock**

**City Construction**: Volcanic stone provided building materials; **Fire Genasi** allies helped establish forges

**Planar Gateway**: Scholars discovered natural planar rifts, built research facility to study them

### Modern Era (400-450 AS)

**Tiefling Refugee Network**: Established safe houses across Aethelgard, funneling refugees to Infernia

**Malakor Shadowdancer**: **Dark Elf** benefactor provides funding through **Shadowfen Collective**

**International Recognition**: **Queen Elara** and **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** recognize Infernia's sovereignty

## Population and Culture

Infernia's population of 15,000 is predominantly **tiefling** (85%), with **Fire Genasi** (10%), **Dark Elf** merchants (3%), and other planar-touched beings (2%). The city celebrates **tiefling** culture while welcoming all persecuted minorities.

### Cultural Practices

**Naming Ceremonies**: Young **tieflings** choose adult names rejecting persecution-era labels

**Planar Festivals**: Annual celebrations of infernal heritage, featuring fire displays and shadow magic

**Refugee Support**: Established **tieflings** mentor newcomers, providing housing and employment

### Religious Diversity

Infernia hosts temples to multiple deities, reflecting **tiefling** spiritual diversity. The **Order of the Silver Dawn** maintains a chapter house, providing healing and advocacy.

## Economy and Trade

Infernia's economy centers on planar research, volcanic crafting, and refugee support services:

### Planar Research

**Planar Gateway** facility attracts scholars studying infernal heritage. **Arcane College of Thaldor** collaborates on research, sharing findings about planar-touched biology.

### Volcanic Crafting

**Fire Genasi** smiths work volcanic materials into weapons and art. **Pyre the Unquenchable** from **Everflame Peak** visits annually to teach advanced techniques.

### Trade Goods

- **Obsidian Weapons**: Volcanic glass blades, popular among **tiefling** warriors
- **Planar Crystals**: Rift-harvested crystals with magical properties
- **Fire-Resistant Cloth**: Woven from volcanic fiber, exported to **Drakkenheim** and **Stonehold**

## Notable Features

### Planar Gateway

Research facility studying natural planar rifts. **Tiefling** scholars map infernal connections, developing treatments for persecution-related trauma. **Arcane College of Thaldor** researchers visit regularly.

### Refugee Safe Houses

**Tiefling Refugee Network** operates underground safe houses across Aethelgard. Infernia serves as the network's headquarters, coordinating rescue operations and resettlement.

### Shadowfen Collective Embassy

**Malakor Shadowdancer**'s **Shadowfen Collective** maintains embassy in Infernia, providing funding and political support. The embassy doubles as **Dark Elf** trade mission.

### Temple of Acceptance

Multi-faith temple welcoming all persecuted minorities. **Order of the Silver Dawn** clerics provide healing and advocacy, working alongside **tiefling** shamans and **Fire Genasi** priests.

## Rulers and Leaders

### Council of Refuge

Infernia governed by elected council of **tiefling** elders, **Fire Genasi** artisans, and **Dark Elf** diplomats. Current High Councilor is **Miria Shadowborn**, a **tiefling** who fled persecution in the **Eastern Kingdoms**.

### Malakor Shadowdancer

**Dark Elf** benefactor and primary funding source. **Malakor Shadowdancer**'s **Shadowfen Collective** provides 60% of Infernia's budget, earning him honorary citizenship.

### Planar Gateway Directors

Scholars leading planar research report to the Council. Current Director is **Vex Infernus**, a **tiefling** mage specializing in infernal genetics.

## Connected Events

### The Great Persecution (380-390 AS)

**Tiefling** persecution across Aethelgard drove refugees to Infernia. **Queen Elara**'s Acceptance Edicts (440 AS) eventually reduced persecution, but Infernia remains a sanctuary.

### Planar Rift Discovery (395 AS)

**Tiefling** scholars discovered natural planar rifts beneath **Refuge Rock**. The **Planar Gateway** facility was built to study these phenomena.

### Shadowfen Alliance (420 AS)

**Malakor Shadowdancer** formalized **Dark Elf** support for Infernia. The alliance provides funding in exchange for **tiefling** intelligence networks.

## Connected Characters

- **Malakor Shadowdancer**: **Dark Elf** benefactor, funds Infernia through **Shadowfen Collective**
- **Miria Shadowborn**: High Councilor of Infernia, former **Eastern Kingdoms** refugee
- **Vex Infernus**: Director of **Planar Gateway**, infernal genetics researcher
- **Pyre the Unquenchable**: **Fire Genasi** smith, teaches volcanic crafting techniques

## Connected Locations

- **Shattered Isles**: Archipelago containing **Refuge Rock** and Infernia
- **Shadowfen**: **Dark Elf** territories, **Malakor Shadowdancer**'s base
- **Everflame Peak**: **Fire Genasi** volcanic site, **Pyre the Unquenchable**'s home
- **Planar Gateway**: Research facility beneath Infernia studying planar rifts

# Sylvaris

## Overview

Sylvaris is a **Wood Elf** forest city in the **Whispering Woods**, **Western Reaches**, grown from living trees through ancient elven magic rather than constructed. The city serves as **Sylvaris Nightbreeze**'s **Forest Wardens** headquarters and represents traditional **Wood Elf** isolation, with minimal contact with surface world. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** respects Sylvaris' autonomy while coordinating border security, and the **Academy of Stars** studies the city's unique arboreal architecture.

## Geography and Climate

Sylvaris nestles deep within the **Whispering Woods**, an ancient forest in the **Western Reaches**. The city exists in harmony with its environment, buildings grown from living trees through elven tree-shaping magic. The climate is temperate, with canopy cover creating mild temperatures year-round.

### Forest Integration

- **Living Structures**: Buildings grown from massive oak and ash trees
- **Canopy Walkways**: Elevated paths connecting tree-dwellings
- **Forest Floor Markets**: Ground-level trading areas for surface visitors
- **Sacred Groves**: Unmodified forest sections for druidic rituals

## History and Founding

Sylvaris was founded in 127 AS by **Wood Elf** clans seeking isolation from surface conflicts. The city's tree-growing magic was developed by ancient elven druids, creating architecture that heals and adapts over centuries.

### Early Period (127-300 AS)

**First Trees**: Founders shaped initial living structures from ancient oaks

**Druidic Council**: Established governance through druid elders and clan representatives

**Isolation Policy**: Limited contact with surface world, trade through intermediaries only

### Modern Era (300-450 AS)

**Forest Wardens**: **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** founded ranger order protecting **Whispering Woods**

**Academy Partnership**: **Academy of Stars** studies arboreal architecture, respects isolation

**Border Security**: **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** coordinates defense without interference

## Population and Culture

Sylvaris' population of 8,000 is predominantly **Wood Elf** (90%), with **Halfling** traders (5%), **Gnome** researchers (3%), and other forest dwellers (2%). The city maintains traditional **Wood Elf** culture with minimal outside influence.

### Cultural Practices

**Tree-Bonding**: Each **Wood Elf** bonds with a tree at birth, their life force connected

**Canopy Festivals**: Seasonal celebrations in high branches, honoring forest spirits

**Silent Communication**: **Wood Elves** developed hand signals for forest hunting

### Spiritual Traditions

Sylvaris worships nature spirits and elven deities through druidic circles. The **Order of the Silver Dawn** maintains limited presence, respecting **Wood Elf** spiritual autonomy.

## Economy and Trade

Sylvaris' economy centers on forest products, ranger services, and limited magical research:

### Forest Products

**Living Wood**: Tree-shaped furniture and structures, exported to **Silvermoon** and **Thaldor**

**Herbal Medicine**: Swamp and forest herbs, traded through **Halfling** intermediaries

**Ranger Gear**: **Forest Wardens** craft superior bows and leather armor

### Trade Restrictions

Sylvaris limits trade to preserve isolation. **Halfling** merchants act as intermediaries, bringing surface goods to forest edges rather than entering the city.

## Notable Features

### Forest Wardens Headquarters

**Sylvaris Nightbreeze**'s ranger order trains in Sylvaris. The headquarters includes archery ranges, tracking grounds, and tree-climbing towers. Rangers patrol **Whispering Woods**, preventing unauthorized logging and hunting.

### Tree-Shaping Groves

Ancient druids teach tree-shaping magic to apprentices. The groves contain partially-formed structures, demonstrating centuries-long architectural projects. **Academy of Stars** researchers study these techniques.

### Sacred Grove of Elders

Unmodified forest section where druid councils meet. The grove contains the oldest trees in **Whispering Woods**, some over 2,000 years old. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** visits annually to consult with druid elders.

### Canopy Market

Elevated trading platform where **Halfling** merchants exchange surface goods for forest products. The market operates weekly, with strict rules against permanent structures.

## Rulers and Leaders

### Druid Council

Sylvaris governed by council of druid elders representing different forest aspects: growth, decay, protection, and balance. Current Council Speaker is **Elder Oakheart**, a 600-year-old **Wood Elf** druid.

### Sylvaris Nightbreeze

**Forest Wardens** commander reports to the Druid Council. **Sylvaris Nightbreeze** coordinates border security with **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s forces while maintaining city isolation.

### Clan Representatives

Major **Wood Elf** clans send representatives to the council, ensuring diverse voices in governance. Clan leadership is hereditary, but council positions are earned through service.

## Connected Events

### Whispering Woods Treaty (425 AS)

**Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** formalized **Silvermoon**-Sylvaris border agreements. The treaty respects **Wood Elf** isolation while coordinating defense against external threats.

### Forest Warden Founding (430 AS)

**Sylvaris Nightbreeze** established ranger order to protect **Whispering Woods**. The Wardens recruit from multiple species but maintain **Wood Elf** leadership.

### Academy Partnership (435 AS)

**Academy of Stars** gained permission to study arboreal architecture. Researchers must respect isolation policies and share findings with Sylvaris first.

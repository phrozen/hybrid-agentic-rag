# Cities of Aethelgard

This index catalogs the urban centers and settlements where the peoples of Aethelgard gather, trade, and build their civilizations.

---

## Major Cities

### Silverhaven
- **Capital of the Eastern Kingdoms**
- **Dominant Race**: Human (60%), with significant Elven, Dwarven, Halfling minorities
- **Population**: 100,000+
- **Notable Features**: Grand Market, Silver Spire, Arcane College of Thaldor
- **Ruler**: Queen Elara (via husband King Aldric's lineage)
- **Founded**: 127 AS by King Aldric the Unifier

### Thaldor
- **Home of the Arcane College**
- **Dominant Race**: Multi-species academic city
- **Notable Features**: Arcane College of Thaldor, Hall of Archmages
- **Founded**: 342 AS (college founding)

### Thronehold
- **Ancient Human Kingdom**
- **Ruler**: Queen Elara
- **Notable Features**: Royal palace, seat of power

## Race-Specific Cities

### Elven Cities
- **Silvermoon** - High Elf capital, ancient city
- **Sylvaris** - Wood Elf forest city, grown from trees
- **Umbralis** - Dark Elf underground city

### Dwarven Cities
- **Stonehold** - Greatest dwarven hold
- **Ironforge** - Trading hold, open to surface merchants
- **Stonehelm** - Hill dwarf diplomatic center

### Other Race Cities
- **Drakkenheim** - Dragonborn mountain capital
- **Grommashar** - Orcish plains capital
- **Clawrest** - Catfolk mountain city
- **Hornhold** - Minotaur mountain capital
- **Elemental Confluence** - Genasi capital
- **Infernia** - Tiefling sanctuary

# Crystal Bay

## Overview

Crystal Bay is a major coastal city and natural harbor in the **Southern Expanse**, serving as the primary trade hub connecting the southern continents with the **Eastern Kingdoms** and **Western Reaches**. Named for the crystal-clear waters of its protected bay, the city has grown from a simple fishing village into a bustling cosmopolitan port where **Dragonborn**, **humans**, **Catfolk**, **Lizardfolk**, and dozens of other species conduct trade worth millions. The city's strategic location, deep-water harbor, and liberal trade policies have made it the jewel of southern commerce.

## Geography and Climate

### Location

**Geographic Position:**
- **Coordinates**: Southern coast of main continent
- **Region**: **Southern Expanse**, north-central coast
- **Bay**: Natural harbor, 3 miles across
- **River**: River Aethel mouth (connects to **Silverhaven**)
- **Area**: 50 square miles (city and suburbs)

**Strategic Advantages:**
- Protected deep-water harbor
- River access to interior
- Central to southern trade routes
- Natural defense position
- Fresh water abundant

### Climate

**Tropical Maritime Climate:**
- **Temperature**: 75-90°F year-round
- **Wet Season** (Months 1-6): Heavy afternoon rains, lush vegetation
- **Dry Season** (Months 7-12): Less rain, trade winds
- **Hurricanes**: Rare, but possible (Months 2-4)
- **Humidity**: High (70-90%)

**Natural Features:**
- **Crystal Bay**: Protected harbor
- **Coral Reefs**: Natural breakwater
- **White Sand Beaches**: Tourism potential
- **Tropical Forest**: Surrounding region
- **River Delta**: Fertile agricultural land

# Thronehold

## Overview

Thronehold is the ancient capital of the Eastern Kingdoms and the seat of power for the human monarchy, currently ruled by **Queen Elara**. One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Aethelgard, Thronehold serves as the political heart of the human kingdoms and a symbol of royal authority that has endured for over four centuries.

## Geography and Climate

Located in the fertile Eastern Plains, approximately 150 miles southwest of **Silverhaven**, Thronehold sits on a strategic hill overlooking the confluence of two rivers. The elevated position provides natural defense and commanding views of the surrounding countryside for miles in every direction.

The climate is temperate continental, with the Eastern Plains providing rich agricultural land that supports the city's large population. The rivers provide fresh water, transportation, and natural defensive barriers on two sides of the city.

## History and Founding

Thronehold was founded in the early Age of Expansion (approximately 50 AS) by human settlers seeking a defensible position in the Eastern Plains. The city grew rapidly, becoming the capital of the most powerful human kingdom in the region.

**King Aldric the Unifier** was born in Thronehold in 95 AS and used it as his base for unifying the Eastern Kingdoms (120-130 AS). After founding **Silverhaven** in 127 AS, Aldric maintained Thronehold as his primary residence and military headquarters.

During the **War of Shattered Skies**, Thronehold served as a major military staging ground. **Queen Elara** led the defense of the city in 446 AS after her husband's death, successfully repelling orcish advances and demonstrating her capabilities as a ruler.

## Population and Culture

Thronehold's population of approximately 80,000 is predominantly human but includes significant minorities of other species:

- **Humans** (75%): Dominant population, control government and military
- **Dwarves** (10%): Metalworkers, engineers, and merchants
- **Elves** (5%): Diplomats and scholars
- **Halflings** (5%): Service industry and agriculture
- **Other Species** (5%): Various representatives and merchants

The culture is formal and hierarchical, reflecting the city's role as royal capital. Court etiquette is strictly observed, and the presence of the monarchy influences all aspects of city life.

## Rulers and Leaders

### Current Monarchy
- **Queen Elara**: Current ruler since 448 AS, widow of King Aldric II
- **Crown Prince Edmund III**: Heir apparent, being trained in statecraft
- **Royal Council**: Multi-species advisory body including:
  - **General Sarah Ironside** (Human) - Military commander
  - **Archbishop Radiant Dawn** (Aasimar) - Spiritual advisor
  - **Spymistress Kira** (Changeling) - Intelligence coordinator
  - Various noble representatives

### City Government
- **Lord Chancellor**: Appointed by the Queen, administers city
- **Noble Council**: Representatives from major houses
- **Guild Masters**: Leaders of trade organizations
- **Military Commanders**: Defense and security

The monarchy holds ultimate authority, but Queen Elara's multi-species council ensures diverse perspectives in governance.

## Architecture

Thronehold's architecture reflects its royal status:

- **Palace**: Grand stone construction with ornate details
- **Noble Mansions**: Impressive but subordinate to palace
- **Military Buildings**: Functional, defensible construction
- **Common Housing**: Practical stone and timber
- **Temples**: Varied styles for different faiths

The city's elevated position makes it visible for miles, the palace towers dominating the skyline as a symbol of royal authority.

## Defense Systems

Thronehold's defenses are formidable:

- **Natural Barriers**: Rivers on two sides, hill position
- **City Walls**: Stone fortifications with towers
- **Citadel**: Inner fortress within the city
- **Standing Army**: Professional royal forces
- **Royal Guard**: Elite protection force
- **Magical Wards**: Enchantments by College mages

The successful defense in 446 AS proved the effectiveness of these systems under **Queen Elara's** leadership.

## Current Challenges

As Thronehold enters the Age of Splendor, it faces several challenges:

- **Reconstruction**: Repairing war damage from siege
- **Succession**: Preparing Crown Prince Edmund for rule
- **Old Nobility**: Traditionalists resistant to multi-species governance
- **Economic Transition**: Converting wartime economy to peace
- **Rival City**: **Silverhaven's** growing influence

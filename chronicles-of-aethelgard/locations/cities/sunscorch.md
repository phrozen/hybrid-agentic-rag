# Sunscorch

## Overview

Sunscorch is a **Dragonborn** desert city in the **Southern Expanse**, built around an oasis and serving as a caravan hub for overland trade routes. The city's heat-resistant architecture and **Fire Genasi** partnerships, particularly with **Pyre the Unquenchable** from **Everflame Peak**, make it a center for volcanic materials and fire-based crafting. **Lady Zara Goldscale** maintains diplomatic presence in Sunscorch, and the city supplies **Drakkenheim** and **Crystal Bay** with exotic desert goods.

## Geography and Climate

Sunscorch occupies a large oasis in the **Southern Expanse** desert, surrounded by endless sand dunes. The oasis provides fresh water from underground aquifers, supporting date palms, desert crops, and heat-adapted livestock. The climate is extremely hot and dry, with daytime temperatures exceeding 120°F in summer.

### Desert Features

- **Great Oasis**: Fresh water source supporting city and agriculture
- **Sand Dunes**: Shifting desert surrounding the city, navigated by **Catfolk** guides
- **Rock Formations**: Natural windbreaks providing shade and defense
- **Underground Cisterns**: Water storage for dry seasons and sieges

## History and Founding

Sunscorch was founded in 358 AS by **Dragonborn** clans seeking to control southern trade routes. The city's location at the oasis made it a natural stopping point for caravans crossing the **Southern Expanse** desert.

### Founding Period (358-400 AS)

**First Caravanserai**: **Dragonborn** merchants established trading post at the oasis

**City Construction**: Heat-resistant stone architecture, inspired by **Drakkenheim** designs

**Trade Route Development**: **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** partnered with **Dragonborn** to establish regular caravan routes

### Modern Era (400-450 AS)

**Fire Genasi Alliance**: **Pyre the Unquenchable** from **Everflame Peak** established volcanic crafting workshops

**Lady Zara Goldscale**: Appointed diplomatic governor to represent **Drakkenheim** interests

**International Trade**: City became major hub for **Southern Expanse** commerce

## Population and Culture

Sunscorch's population of 18,000 is predominantly **Dragonborn** (60%), **Fire Genasi** (15%), **Catfolk** traders (12%), **Human** merchants (8%), and other species (5%). The city celebrates **Dragonborn** martial traditions while embracing cosmopolitan trade culture.

### Cultural Practices

**Desert Festivals**: Seasonal celebrations marking cool season onset, featuring camel races and fire displays

**Caravan Blessings**: **Dragonborn** shamans bless merchant caravans before desert crossings

**Water Ceremonies**: Ritual appreciation of oasis water, recognizing its life-giving importance

### Martial Traditions

Sunscorch maintains **Dragonborn** warrior culture, with city guards trained in desert combat. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s military advisors ensure Sunscorch can defend trade routes from raiders.

## Economy and Trade

Sunscorch's economy centers on caravan trade, volcanic crafting, and desert agriculture:

### Caravan Trade

**Southern Expanse Routes**: **Catfolk** guides lead caravans between Sunscorch, **Crystal Bay**, and **Drakkenheim**. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**'s trading company maintains headquarters in the city.

**Trade Goods**:
- **Desert Spices**: Exotic flavorings from southern oases
- **Volcanic Materials**: **Fire Genasi**-crafted items from **Everflame Peak**
- **Dragonborn Artwork**: Scaled metalwork and scale-mail armor
- **Date Products**: Oasis-grown dates and date wine

### Volcanic Crafting

**Pyre the Unquenchable**'s workshops produce fire-enchanted weapons and heat-resistant materials. **Dragonborn** smiths collaborate with **Fire Genasi**, combining metallurgical skill with elemental magic.

### Desert Agriculture

Oasis farms produce dates, desert grains, and heat-adapted vegetables. **Halfling** agricultural advisors introduced irrigation techniques increasing crop yields.

## Notable Features

### Great Caravanserai

Massive trading compound where merchants warehouse goods, rest camels, and negotiate deals. The Caravanserai includes stables, warehouses, inns, and bargaining halls. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** owns the largest section.

### Pyre's Workshops

**Fire Genasi** master smith **Pyre the Unquenchable** maintains volcanic crafting facilities. The workshops produce legendary fire-enchanted weapons sought by heroes across Aethelgard.

### Oasis Gardens

Terraced gardens surrounding the oasis, featuring date palms, desert flowers, and cooling fountains. The gardens provide food, shade, and beauty in the harsh desert environment.

### Dragonborn Governor's Palace

**Lady Zara Goldscale**'s diplomatic representative rules from this fortified palace. The palace includes audience halls, military command centers, and **Dragonborn** ancestral shrines.

## Rulers and Leaders

### Dragonborn Governor

Sunscorch governed by **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s appointed governor, currently **Commander Scalestorm**, a veteran **Dragonborn** warrior. The governor manages trade, defense, and diplomatic relations.

### Merchant Council

Major trading companies, including **Whiskerwind Trading Company**, advise the governor on commercial matters. The council ensures trade policies benefit all merchants.

### Fire Genasi Elders

**Pyre the Unquenchable** and senior **Fire Genasi** craftsmen form advisory council on volcanic crafting and elemental matters. Their expertise attracts merchants seeking fire-enchanted goods.

## Connected Events

### Southern Trade Route Establishment (365 AS)

**Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** and **Dragonborn** clans formalized caravan routes through **Southern Expanse**. Sunscorch became the primary stopping point.

### Fire Genasi Alliance (425 AS)

**Pyre the Unquenchable** established workshops in Sunscorch, bringing volcanic crafting expertise. The alliance transformed the city into a crafting center.

### Desert Raider Defeat (440 AS)

**Lady Zara Goldscale**'s forces, based in Sunscorch, defeated major raider coalition threatening trade routes. The victory secured **Southern Expanse** commerce for decades.

## Connected Characters

- **Lady Zara Goldscale**: **Dragonborn** leader, appoints Sunscorch's governor
- **Pyre the Unquenchable**: **Fire Genasi** master smith, operates workshops in Sunscorch
- **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind**: **Catfolk** trader, maintains headquarters in Sunscorch
- **Commander Scalestorm**: **Dragonborn** governor, veteran warrior

## Connected Locations

- **Southern Expanse**: Desert region containing Sunscorch
- **Drakkenheim**: **Dragonborn** capital, **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s seat
- **Everflame Peak**: **Fire Genasi** volcanic site, **Pyre the Unquenchable**'s home
- **Crystal Bay**: Port city connected to Sunscorch via caravan routes

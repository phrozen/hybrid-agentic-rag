# Magic of Aethelgard

This index catalogs the magical systems, schools, traditions, and practices that define arcane power in the world of Aethelgard.

---

## Schools of Magic

The eight traditional schools of magic, as taught at the **Arcane College of Thaldor**:

### Abjuration
- **Focus**: Protection, wards, defensive magic
- **Notable Practitioners**: Throndor the Wise (signature: Throndor's Mirror Ward)
- **Applications**: Personal protection, fortress wards, magical barriers

### Conjuration
- **Focus**: Summoning creatures, creating objects, teleportation
- **Applications**: Combat summons, material creation, transportation

### Divination
- **Focus**: Scrying, prediction, knowledge acquisition
- **Applications**: Intelligence gathering, future sight, truth detection

### Enchantment
- **Focus**: Mind-affecting magic, charms, compulsions
- **Applications**: Diplomacy, crowd control, mental influence

### Evocation
- **Focus**: Elemental energy, fire, lightning, force
- **Applications**: Combat magic, elemental manipulation, energy projection

### Illusion
- **Focus**: Deception, phantasms, invisibility
- **Notable Traditions**: Gnomish illusion, Forest Elf natural illusions
- **Applications**: Stealth, misdirection, entertainment

### Necromancy
- **Focus**: Death, undeath, life force manipulation
- **Applications**: Healing, undead creation, life drain

### Transmutation
- **Focus**: Transformation, alteration, polymorphing
- **Applications**: Shape change, material transformation, enhancement

## Racial Magical Traditions

### Elven Magic
- **Characteristics**: Ancient, woven, nature-integrated, long-term
- **Specialties**: High Elf arcane mastery, Wood Elf nature magic, Dark Elf shadow magic

### Dwarven Runemagic
- **Characteristics**: Inscribed, permanent, industrial applications
- **Specialties**: Metal enchantment, stone runes, defensive wards

### Gnomish Illusion
- **Characteristics**: Technical, precise, mechanical integration
- **Specialties**: Illusion technology, automated magical systems

### Human Adaptive Magic
- **Characteristics**: Systematic, practical, diverse sources
- **Specialties**: Integration of multiple traditions, practical applications

### Orcish Shamanism
- **Characteristics**: Spiritual, ancestral, practical
- **Specialties**: Battlefield magic, healing, ancestral communion

### Dragonborn Draconic Magic
- **Characteristics**: Bloodline-based, breath weapon enhancement
- **Specialties**: Innate sorcery, elemental breath refinement

### Tiefling Infernal Magic
- **Characteristics**: Planar, fire-based, shadow
- **Specialties**: Innate spell-like abilities, fiendish pacts

### Aasimar Celestial Magic
- **Characteristics**: Divine, healing, protective
- **Specialties**: Healing abilities, protective wards, celestial transformation

### Genasi Elemental Magic
- **Characteristics**: Direct elemental channeling
- **Specialties**: Fire, water, earth, or air specialization

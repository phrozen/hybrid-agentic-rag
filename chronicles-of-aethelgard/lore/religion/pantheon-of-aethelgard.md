# The Pantheon of Aethelgard

## Overview

The Pantheon of Aethelgard encompasses the diverse deities worshipped across the continent, with different species venerating different divine powers while the **Order of the Silver Dawn** promotes multi-faith cooperation. Major deities include Moradin (dwarven creator), Corellon Larethian (elven protector), and various human, **Dragonborn**, and **Lizardfolk** gods. The **Order of the Sacred Anvil** serves Moradin in **Stonehold**, while the **Order of the Radiant Dawn** protects **aasimar** and **tiefling** planar connections.

## Major Deities by Species

Aethelgard's pantheon reflects the continent's species diversity, with each culture venerating deities reflecting their values and needs:

### Dwarven Deities

**Moradin (The Soul Forger)**: Chief dwarven deity, creator of dwarves and patron of craftsmanship, smithing, and family. **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge** serves as Moradin's high priest in **Stonehold**'s **Temple of the Sacred Anvil**.

**Domains**: Craftsmanship, Smithing, Family, Protection, Earth

**Worshippers**: All dwarves, **Ironhand Guild** members, smiths, miners

**Temples**: **Stonehold Temple of the Sacred Anvil**, **Thunder Pass** shrine, **Stormpeak Mountains** cavern temples

**Holy Symbols**: Hammer and anvil, mountain peaks, forged weapons

### Elven Deities

**Corellon Larethian (The Archfey)**: Chief elven deity, creator of elves and patron of magic, arts, and nature. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** claims divine mandate from Corellon to rule **Silvermoon**.

**Domains**: Magic, Arts, Nature, Protection, Beauty

**Worshippers**: **High Elves**, **Wood Elves**, **Dark Elves** (despite exile), mages, artists

**Temples**: **Silvermoon** temples, **Sylvaris** sacred groves, **Whispering Woods** shrines

**Holy Symbols**: Crescent moon, stars, living trees, arcane symbols

### Human Deities

**Pelor (The Shining One)**: Chief human deity in the **Eastern Kingdoms**, god of sun, agriculture, healing, and time. **Queen Elara** attends Pelor's temples for royal ceremonies.

**Domains**: Sun, Agriculture, Healing, Time, Justice

**Worshippers**: Humans across **Eastern Kingdoms**, farmers, healers, merchants

**Temples**: **Silverhaven Grand Temple**, **Thronehold Royal Chapel**, **Eastern Plains** village shrines

**Holy Symbols**: Sun disk, golden light, wheat sheaves

### Dragonborn Deities

**Bahamut (The Platinum Dragon)**: Chief **Dragonborn** deity, god of justice, protection, and noble dragonkind. **Lady Zara Goldscale** maintains Bahamut's temples in **Drakkenheim** and **Sunscorch**.

**Domains**: Justice, Protection, Dragons, Honor, Leadership

**Worshippers**: **Dragonborn**, metallic dragon worshippers, paladins, knights

**Temples**: **Drakkenheim Dragon Temples**, **Sunscorch** shrines, **Crystal Bay** **Dragonborn** district

**Holy Symbols**: Platinum dragon, dragon scales, justice scales

### Lizardfolk Spirits

**Nature Spirits**: **Lizardfolk** worship ancestral and nature spirits rather than personified deities. **Chief Ssithik**'s **Shaman Vessska** communicates with these spirits for guidance.

**Domains**: Nature, Ancestors, Survival, Hunting, Water

**Worshippers**: **Lizardfolk**, swamp dwellers, hunters, shamans

**Temples**: **Ssithik Swamp** sacred sites, **Shadowfen** spirit groves, **Aqualonis** ancestral shrines

**Holy Symbols**: Animal totems, ancestral skulls, swamp plants

### Genasi Elemental Powers

**Elemental Lords**: **Genasi** venerate the elemental powers of Fire, Water, Earth, and Air rather than personified deities. **Elemental Confluence** temples honor all four elements equally.

**Domains**: Elements, Nature, Change, Balance, Power

**Worshippers**: **Fire Genasi**, **Water Genasi**, **Earth Genasi**, **Air Genasi**

**Temples**: **Elemental Confluence** central temple, **Everflame Peak** fire shrines, **Crystal Bay** water temples

**Holy Symbols**: Elemental symbols (flame, wave, mountain, wind)

## Religious Organizations

### Order of the Silver Dawn

**Multi-Faith Cooperation**: **Queen Elara**'s royal charter established the **Order of the Silver Dawn** to promote interfaith dialogue and prevent persecution. The Order operates temples serving all deities equally.

**Headquarters**: **Silverhaven Grand Temple**

**Leadership**: **Archbishop Radiant Dawn** (**Aasimar** high priest)

**Mission**: Prevent religious persecution, coordinate humanitarian aid, train multi-faith clerics

**Achievements**: Ended **The Great Persecution** of **tieflings**, established refugee safe houses, coordinated plague relief

### Order of the Sacred Anvil

**Dwarven Faith**: **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge** leads this orthodox dwarven religious order serving Moradin. The Order maintains **Stonehold** temples and blesses **Ironhand Guild** runemagic.

**Headquarters**: **Stonehold Temple of the Sacred Anvil**

**Leadership**: **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge**

**Mission**: Preserve dwarven religious traditions, bless craftsmanship, advise **King Thorin Stoneforge**

**Achievements**: Maintained faith through **War of Shattered Skies**, blessed **Silverhaven** defensive wards, trained dwarf priests

### Order of the Radiant Dawn

**Planar Protection**: Subsidiary of the **Order of the Silver Dawn** protecting **aasimar** and **tiefling** rights. **Radiant Dawn** (**Aasimar** healer) leads this order from **Silverhaven**.

**Headquarters**: **Silverhaven Temple of Light**

**Leadership**: **Radiant Dawn** (**Aasimar** high priestess)

**Mission**: Protect planar-touched beings, provide healing, advocate for acceptance

**Achievements**: Secured **Queen Elara**'s Acceptance Edicts, established **Infernia** refugee support, trained **aasimar** clerics

## Religious History

### The Great Persecution (380-390 AS)

**Tiefling Persecution**: Widespread discrimination against **tieflings** across Aethelgard, driven by fear of infernal heritage. **Tieflings** fled to **Infernia** sanctuary.

**Order Intervention**: **Order of the Silver Dawn** documented atrocities, provided refuge, lobbied **Queen Elara** for intervention.

**Acceptance Edicts** (440 AS): **Queen Elara**'s royal decree granting **tiefling** rights, influenced by **Order of the Silver Dawn** advocacy.

### War of Shattered Skies Religious Impact (440-450 AS)

**Interfaith Cooperation**: Clerics from all faiths coordinated battlefield healing and refugee relief. **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge** blessed dwarven troops; **Order of the Silver Dawn** healed all species equally.

**Post-War Reconciliation**: Religious leaders participated in **Treaty of Silverhaven** negotiations, ensuring freedom of worship provisions.

**Reconstruction**: **Order of the Silver Dawn** coordinated temple rebuilding in war-torn regions. **Queen Elara** funded multi-faith temples as symbols of unity.

## Religious Practices and Festivals

### Multi-Faith Festivals

**Festival of Lights** (Winter Solstice): Celebrated across **Eastern Kingdoms**, honoring light deities. **Queen Elara** attends **Silverhaven Grand Temple** ceremonies. **Order of the Silver Dawn** coordinates multi-faith participation.

**Day of Remembrance** (Post-**Sundering** Anniversary): All faiths honor the dead from **the Sundering** and **War of Shattered Skies**. **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**, **King Thorin Stoneforge**, and **Queen Elara** attend joint ceremonies.

### Species-Specific Festivals

**Dwarven Forge Festival** (**Stonehold**): **Ironhand Guild** blesses new forges. **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge** performs sacred rituals. **King Thorin Stoneforge** attends.

**Elven Moon Festival** (**Silvermoon**): **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper** leads ceremonies at **The Moonwell**. **High Elves** honor Corellon and ancestral spirits.

**Dragonborn Honor Day** (**Drakkenheim**): **Lady Zara Goldscale** leads Bahamut temple ceremonies. **Dragonborn** warriors renew oaths of protection.

## Governance and Regulation

### Royal Protection

**Queen Elara**: Grants royal charter to the **Order of the Silver Dawn**, funds multi-faith temples, prosecutes religious persecution.

**King Thorin Stoneforge**: Protects dwarven religious orthodoxy, funds **Temple of the Sacred Anvil**, consults **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge**.

**Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**: Maintains elven religious autonomy, cooperates on interfaith initiatives, respects **Wood Elf** spiritual independence.

### International Agreements

**Freedom of Worship Treaty** (450 AS): **Treaty of Silverhaven** provision guaranteeing religious freedom across signatory nations. **Order of the Silver Dawn** monitors compliance.

**Clerical Exchange Program**: **Arcane College of Thaldor** hosts interfaith theological conferences. **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge**, **Order of the Silver Dawn** leaders, and elven priests participate.

# Bahamut and the Dragonborn Faith

## Overview

Bahamut the Platinum Dragon is the chief deity of **Dragonborn** religion, representing justice, protection, and noble dragonkind. **Lady Zara Goldscale** and the **Dragonborn Clans** of **Drakkenheim** maintain Bahamut's temples throughout the **Southern Expanse**. Bahamut's teachings emphasize honor, protection of the weak, and resistance to tyranny, influencing **Dragonborn** participation in the **Minotaur Naval Alliance** and anti-piracy efforts.

## Bahamut's Domains and Portfolio

Bahamut governs aspects of existence central to **Dragonborn** culture and values:

### Justice

**Divine Judgment**: Bahamut judges the dead, weighing souls against platinum standards. **Dragonborn** believe virtuous souls join Bahamut in celestial dragon realms.

**Mortal Justice**: **Dragonborn** legal systems reflect Bahamut's emphasis on fair judgment. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s courts use Bahamut's symbols, and judges swear oaths before Bahamut's altars.

**Tyranny Opposition**: Bahamut's followers actively resist tyrannical rulers. **Lady Zara Goldscale** supported **Queen Elara** against orcish tyranny during the **War of Shattered Skies**.

### Protection

**Defensive Warfare**: Bahamut blesses defensive wars and protection of innocents. **Dragonborn** warriors pray to Bahamut before battle. **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s **Dragonborn** naval officers invoke Bahamut's protection.

**Weak Protection**: Bahamut's clergy provides sanctuary to refugees and persecution victims. **Drakkenheim** temples sheltered **tiefling** refugees during **The Great Persecution**.

**Oath Keeping**: Bahamut enforces oaths and promises. **Dragonborn** consider oath-breaking the gravest sin. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s treaties are sworn before Bahamut's altars.

### Dragonkind

**Metallic Dragons**: Bahamut rules over good dragons (gold, silver, bronze, copper, brass). **Dragonborn** revere metallic dragons as Bahamut's mortal servants.

**Dragonborn Creation**: **Dragonborn** believe Bahamut created their race from dragon essence. **Dragonborn Ancestral Halls** in **Drakkenheim** depict creation myths.

**Dragon Worship**: Bahamut accepts worship from both dragons and **Dragonborn**. Ancient metallic dragons occasionally visit **Drakkenheim** temples.

## Worship and Rituals

**Dragonborn** religious practices honor Bahamut through martial discipline, community service, and ancestral veneration:

### Daily Prayers

**Dawn Observances**: **Dragonborn** warriors greet dawn with prayers to Bahamut, asking for strength to protect the weak. **Lady Zara Goldscale** leads dawn prayers in **Drakkenheim**'s main temple.

**Dusk Reflections**: Evening prayers examine the day's actions against Bahamut's justice standards. **Dragonborn** confess failures and vow improvement.

### Temple Ceremonies

**Dragon Temples**: **Drakkenheim**, **Sunscorch**, and **Crystal Bay** feature grand Bahamut temples with platinum dragon statues. **High Priest Goldscale** (**Lady Zara Goldscale**'s cousin) oversees **Drakkenheim** temples.

**Oath Ceremonies**: **Dragonborn** warriors, leaders, and parents swear oaths before Bahamut's altars. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s coronation included Bahamut oath-swearing.

**Blessings**: Bahamut's clerics bless weapons, armor, ships, and children. **Admiral Hornbreaker** requested Bahamut blessings for his flagship.

### Annual Festivals

**Platinum Day** (Spring Equinox): **Dragonborn** celebrate Bahamut's creation of dragonkind. **Lady Zara Goldscale** leads processions through **Drakkenheim** streets.

**Justice Festival** (Summer Solstice): Courts release reformed prisoners; communities reconcile disputes. **Dragonborn** Clans resolve conflicts through Bahamut-mediated arbitration.

**Protection Vigil** (Autumn Equinox): Warriors renew oaths to protect the weak. **Dragonborn** Clans conduct military drills honoring Bahamut.

## Temples and Holy Sites

### Drakkenheim Dragon Temples

**Main Temple**: Largest Bahamut temple in Aethelgard, built into **Drakkenheim**'s mountain cliffs. **High Priest Goldscale** leads 100 priests and paladins. **Lady Zara Goldscale** funds temple operations.

**Features**:
- Platinum dragon statue (50 feet tall)
- Warrior training grounds
- Refugee shelter wings
- Oath-swearing halls
- **Dragonborn Ancestral Halls** connection

### Sunscorch Bahamut Shrine

**Desert Temple**: **Dragonborn** caravan hub temple serving travelers. **Pyre the Unquenchable** visits annually to bless fire-enchanted weapons before Bahamut's altar.

**Functions**:
- Caravan blessings
- Traveler hospitality
- Desert justice court
- **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s diplomatic outpost

### Crystal Bay Dragon District Temple

**Port Temple**: Serves **Dragonborn** merchants and sailors. **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s **Dragonborn** naval officers worship here before voyages.

**Services**:
- Sailor blessings
- Maritime justice
- Merchant dispute resolution
- **Mayor Ironscale** interfaith cooperation

## Clergy and Orders

### Bahamut's Clergy

**Priests**: Heal wounds, bless weapons, counsel the faithful. **High Priest Goldscale** leads **Drakkenheim** clergy. **Order of the Silver Dawn** coordinates with Bahamut priests on humanitarian projects.

**Paladins**: Holy warriors smiting tyranny and protecting innocents. **Lady Zara Goldscale** commands a personal guard of Bahamut paladins. **Queen Elara** employs Bahamut paladins in her armies.

**Monks**: Ascetic warriors perfecting body and spirit through Bahamut's teachings. **Dragonborn** monasteries train monks in martial arts and meditation.

### Knight Orders

**Order of the Platinum Dragon**: Elite **Dragonborn** paladins serving **Lady Zara Goldscale** directly. The Order participated in the **War of Shattered Skies**, defending **Silverhaven**.

**Protectors of the Weak**: Roaming paladins righting wrongs across the **Southern Expanse**. **Merchant Prince Whiskerwind** employs Protectors as caravan guards.

**Justice Bringers**: Inquisitors hunting tyrants and oathbreakers. **Chief Ssithik** cooperates with Justice Bringers to combat **Lizardfolk** slavers.

## Sacred Texts and Symbols

### The Platinum Codex

**Holy Book**: Bahamut's teachings recorded in the Platinum Codex, kept in **Drakkenheim**'s main temple. **High Priest Goldscale** interprets the Codex for **Lady Zara Goldscale**.

**Key Teachings**:
- Protect the weak and innocent
- Judge fairly and mercifully
- Resist tyranny in all forms
- Keep oaths sacred
- Honor dragonkind heritage

### Holy Symbols

**Platinum Dragon**: Primary symbol depicting Bahamut as platinum dragon. **Dragonborn** warriors tattoo the symbol on scales or armor.

**Justice Scales**: Representing Bahamut's judgment. **Dragonborn** courts display justice scales.

**Dragon Egg**: Symbolizing **Dragonborn** creation and rebirth. **Dragonborn** parents bless hatchlings before dragon egg altars.

## Relations with Other Faiths

### Order of the Silver Dawn

**Cooperation**: Bahamut clergy participate actively in the **Order of the Silver Dawn**. **Lady Zara Goldscale** funds the Order's **Silverhaven Grand Temple**. **Archbishop Radiant Dawn** coordinates humanitarian projects with Bahamut priests.

**Interfaith Dialogues**: **High Priest Goldscale** attends annual conferences with **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge** (Moradin), **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s priests (Corellon), and **Order of the Silver Dawn** leaders.

### Elven Faiths

**Respectful Distance**: Bahamut worshippers respect **Lady Celestia Moonwhisper**'s Corellon faith. Theological differences exist but rarely cause conflict. **Wood Elf** druids in **Whispering Woods** cooperate with Bahamut rangers on forest protection.

### Dwarven Faith

**Strong Alliance**: Bahamut and Moradin worshippers maintain close ties. **King Thorin Stoneforge** and **Lady Zara Goldscale** coordinate religious festivals. **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge** and **High Priest Goldscale** are personal friends.

### Lizardfolk Spirits

**Practical Cooperation**: **Chief Ssithik**'s shamanic traditions differ from Bahamut worship, but both faiths support **Lizardfolk**-**Dragonborn** trade agreements. **Shaman Vessska** and Bahamut priests respect each other's spiritual practices.

## Cultural Impact

### Dragonborn Society

**Legal Systems**: **Dragonborn** Clans base laws on Bahamut's justice principles. **Lady Zara Goldscale**'s courts reference the Platinum Codex.

**Military Doctrine**: **Dragonborn** warriors fight defensively, protecting allies. **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s **Dragonborn** naval officers follow Bahamut's protection teachings.

**Diplomatic Relations**: **Dragonborn** treaties include Bahamut oath-clauses. **Queen Elara** respects **Dragonborn** oaths, knowing Bahamut enforces them.

### Multi-Species Influence

**Human Adoption**: Some **Eastern Kingdoms** humans worship Bahamut alongside Pelor. **Queen Elara** tolerates Bahamut worship, appreciating justice and protection teachings.

**Paladin Training**: **Order of the Silver Dawn** trains paladins in Bahamut traditions. **Aasimar** paladins often gravitate toward Bahamut's justice portfolio.

**Anti-Piracy**: **Admiral Hornbreaker**'s anti-piracy campaigns receive Bahamut blessings. **Dragonborn** naval officers view piracy as Bahamut-forbidden tyranny.

## Connected Characters

- **Lady Zara Goldscale**: **Dragonborn** leader, chief Bahamut worshipper
- **High Priest Goldscale**: **Drakkenheim** temple leader, **Lady Zara**'s cousin
- **Queen Elara**: Human ruler, respects Bahamut oaths
- **Admiral Hornbreaker**: **Minotaur** naval commander, cooperates with **Dragonborn** Bahamut officers
- **Archbishop Radiant Dawn**: **Order of the Silver Dawn** leader, interfaith partner
- **High Priest Thrain Sacredforge**: Dwarven religious leader, Bahamut-Moradin cooperation

## Connected Locations

- **Drakkenheim**: **Dragonborn** capital, Bahamut temple headquarters
- **Sunscorch**: **Dragonborn** desert city, Bahamut shrine
- **Crystal Bay**: Port city, **Dragonborn** district temple
- **Dragonborn Ancestral Halls**: **Drakkenheim** site depicting Bahamut creation myths
- **Silverhaven Grand Temple**: **Order of the Silver Dawn** headquarters, Bahamut altar included

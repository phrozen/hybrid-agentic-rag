## Planar Powers

### Celestial Powers
- **Connected to**: Aasimar heritage
- **Domains**: Justice, protection, healing, redemption
- **Worshippers**: Aasimar, paladins, clerics of good deities

### Infernal Powers
- **Connected to**: Tiefling heritage
- **Domains**: Fire, shadow, ambition, transformation
- **Worshippers**: Tieflings, warlocks, some humans

### Elemental Forces
- **Connected to**: Genasi heritage
- **Domains**: Fire, water, earth, air
- **Worshippers**: Genasi, druids, elemental shamans

## Religious Organizations

### Order of the Silver Dawn
- **Type**: Multi-faith religious order
- **Founded**: 320 AS
- **Headquarters**: The Radiant Temple, Silverhaven
- **Principles**: Acceptance of all faiths, humanitarian service
- **Notable Members**: Archbishop Radiant Dawn (Aasimar high priest)
- **Significance**: Accepts Tieflings, Lycanthropes, and other marginalized groups

### Order of the Sacred Anvil
- **Type**: Dwarven craft deity order
- **Principles**: Craftsmanship as worship, ancestral honor
- **Significance**: Gained followers among other species

### Order of the Golden Hearth
- **Type**: Halfling hospitality order
- **Principles**: Hospitality as sacred duty, community wellness
- **Significance**: Spread to other species, operates inns and community centers

### Order of the Golden Gear
- **Type**: Gnomish religious-scientific order
- **Principles**: Understanding creation through invention
- **Significance**: Blends religion and science

### Spirit Walkers
- **Type**: Orcish shamanic order
- **Principles**: Ancestral communion, spiritual guidance
- **Notable Members**: Shaman Ner'zhul (legendary spirit walker)
- **Significance**: Advisors to chieftains, keepers of oral history

### The Radiant Temple

- **Dedicated to**: Multiple faiths

### Dwarven Temple-Holds
- **Location**: Various dwarven holds
- **Dedicated to**: Craft deities and ancestors

### Elven Sacred Groves
- **Location**: Western Reaches forests
- **Dedicated to**: Nature deities
- **Significance**: Natural worship sites, magical focal points

## Religious Practices

### Ancestral Worship
- **Practiced by**: Dwarves, Orcs, Dragonborn, Elves
- **Methods**: Offerings, ceremonies, maintaining genealogies
- **Significance**: Connection to deceased relatives and clan founders

### Nature Worship
- **Practiced by**: Elves, Halflings, Lizardfolk, Forest Gnomes
- **Methods**: Seasonal rituals, environmental stewardship
- **Significance**: Harmony with natural world

### Celestial Communion
- **Practiced by**: Aasimar, some humans
- **Methods**: Prayer, meditation, divine service
- **Significance**: Direct connection to celestial powers

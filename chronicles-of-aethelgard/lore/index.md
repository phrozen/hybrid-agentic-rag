## Magic Systems

### Overview of Magic in Aethelgard

Magic permeates the world of Aethelgard, manifesting in different forms across species and cultures. The **Arcane College of Thaldor** serves as the premier institution for magical study, bringing together diverse magical traditions.

### Schools of Magic

*To be expanded with individual school entries*

- **Abjuration** - Protective magic, wards, and defenses
- **Conjuration** - Summoning and creation magic
- **Divination** - Scrying, prediction, and knowledge magic
- **Enchantment** - Mind-affecting and charm magic
- **Evocation** - Elemental and energy magic
- **Illusion** - Deception and phantasm magic
- **Necromancy** - Death, undeath, and life force magic
- **Transmutation** - Transformation and alteration magic

### Racial Magical Traditions

- **Elven Magic** - Ancient, woven, nature-integrated
- **Dwarven Runemagic** - Inscribed, permanent, industrial
- **Gnomish Illusion** - Technical, precise, mechanical
- **Human Adaptive Magic** - Systematic, practical, diverse
- **Orcish Shamanism** - Spiritual, ancestral, practical
- **Dragonborn Draconic Magic** - Bloodline-based, breath weapon enhancement
- **Tiefling Infernal Magic** - Planar, fire-based, shadow
- **Aasimar Celestial Magic** - Divine, healing, protective
- **Genasi Elemental Magic** - Direct elemental channeling
- **Lycanthrope Lunar Magic** - Transformation, natural cycles

### Magical Institutions

- **Arcane College of Thaldor** - Premier academy, founded 342 AS by **Throndor the Wise**
- **High Elf Academies** - Ancient elven magical schools
- **Dwarven Runemaster Circles** - Traditional dwarven magical training
- **Orcish Shamanic Traditions** - Oral magical knowledge

## Religion and Pantheons

## Historical Knowledge

### Recorded History

The history of Aethelgard is divided into distinct eras, marked by major events and cosmic changes.

### Ancient Wisdom

- **Elven Historical Memory** - Millennia of continuous record-keeping
- **Dwarven Genealogies** - Detailed clan histories
- **Orcish Oral Traditions** - Epic poems preserving centuries of history
- **Human Written Records** - Systematic documentation

### Cultural Memory

Each species maintains its own historical traditions:

- **High Elves**: Written and magical memory spanning thousands of years
- **Dwarves**: Stone-carved records in underground halls
- **Orcs**: Epic poems and shamanic visions
- **Halflings**: Oral histories and community storytelling
- **Gnomes**: Academic archives and technical documentation
- **Humans**: Written chronicles and institutional memory
